## Definition

"**Orbs launching orbs**" is a descriptor coined by federal LE witnesses (USPER1–USPER6) during the 2025 [[entities/western-us-helicopter-orb-2025]] event for sequential flare-up formations in which a primary [[concepts/orb]] appears to release or generate a series of smaller orbs at high speed. The descriptor was repeated **5+ times** by independent witnesses in the slide deck and supporting USPER statement, suggesting either a real distinct phenomenon or — at minimum — a shared visual percept across multiple trained observers.

## Characteristics

- Multi-orb sequential appearance in close formation, sometimes described as a "string" or "row" of bright points.
- Witnessed at NVG-relevant illumination conditions; some observations describe partial transparency of host orbs.
- Distinct from the simpler [[concepts/multiple-object-formation]] descriptor: orbs-launching-orbs implies a parent-child generative relationship, not just spatial co-presence.
- Possible mundane analogies: aerial-decoy-launch profiles (e.g., MJU flare dispenser), drone-swarm release from a mothership platform, atmospheric optical effects (mirage / lens flare). None comfortably fits the witness chain.

## Documented Examples

- **October 2023 Western U.S. Event** — Six federal LE agents across three teams reported "orbs launching orbs" over two days near a sensitive national security site. See [[summaries/dow-uap-d077-unresolved-case-analysis-update-western-us]] for AARO's analysis and individual witness narratives:
  - [[summaries/dow-uap-d079-narrative-1-western-us-event]] — Witness 1: Orange orb released three red lights; two repetitions with different ejection patterns (vertical alignment on second; horizontal on first)
  - [[summaries/dow-uap-d080-narrative-2-western-us-event]] — Witness 2: Bright orb with smaller red orb *within* it, expelled 3-4 red lights that formed coordinated horizontal formations; process repeated ~5 times in 30 min; red lights hovered for 3+ hours
  - [[summaries/dow-uap-d081-narrative-3-western-us-event]] — Witness 3: Orange orb alternating clear/orange, expelled 3 red orbs mechanically (described as "grapes being expelled from a basketball")
  - [[summaries/dow-uap-d082-narrative-4-western-us-event]] — Witness 4: Orbs "hatching" smaller orbs — 5+ repetitions; smaller lights traveled downward "like a falling flare"; nine-light static horizontal line formation above ridgeline
  - [[summaries/dow-uap-d083-narrative-5-western-us-event]] — Witness 5: Large red orb released 2 smaller red orbs simultaneously (one horizontal, one upward); white flash accompanied
- The 2025 Western U.S. helicopter event — see [[entities/western-us-helicopter-orb-2025]]
