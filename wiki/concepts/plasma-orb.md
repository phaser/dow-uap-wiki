## Definition

**Plasma orb** is the FBI agent's own descriptive term — used in official FD-1057 documentation — for the anomalous luminous spherical objects repeatedly observed near a Northeastern US residence between November 2021 and October 2024. The terminology distinguishes these sightings from simple point-source lights, suggesting the FBI investigator perceived a substantive, self-luminous spherical phenomenon.

## Characteristics

- **Appearance**: Spherical, luminous objects described as "orbs" of various colors (white, red, plasma-like)
- **Behavior**: Hovering in static position, moving laterally, morphing; observed above horizon, close to ground, within trees, and in/near water
- **Proximity**: As close as 200 yards from observer residence
- **Associated phenomena**: Gamma radiation spikes correlated with sighting times; unusual effects on electronics and GPS devices
- **Recording difficulties**: Witness reported common video corruption when attempting to record sightings
- **Duration of pattern**: First observed November 2021, continuing through at least October 2024 — a three-year recurring pattern

## Documented Examples

- **Northeastern US Recurrent Orb Sightings (2021–2024)** — Single witness with hundreds of observations over three years; corroborated by neighbor sightings, FBI Special Agent direct observations (November 2024), and sensor data. - **Northeastern US Dual Red Orbs (2025–2026)** — Two one-meter red orbs with central "white plasma sun," observed by a couple. See [[summaries/fbi-uap-d009-northeastern-orb-2026]] and [[summaries/fbi-uap-d010-northeastern-orb-2026]].
