## Definition

A **saucer hoax** is a UFO report later determined to be a deliberate fabrication — falsified witness account, misrepresented industrial slag offered as alien material, doctored photograph, etc. The category was important to early FBI and Air Force investigators, who used hoaxes as a control to discriminate genuine reports.

## Characteristics

- The most famous early example is the [[entities/maury-island-affair]] — eventually classified by the Air Force as a fabrication by Crisman and Dahl (with the Maury Island "slag" identified as ordinary industrial smelter residue). Whether the Maury Island case was *fully* a hoax remains contested; the chain of custody for the actual samples was destroyed in the Kelso B-25 crash.
- Hoaxers' motives in the corpus include publicity, financial gain, political messaging, and — in a handful of contactee cases — sincere religious belief misclassified by investigators as deliberate fabrication.
