## Definition

A **translucent kite** is a morphology descriptor used by multiple federal law enforcement special agents during the October 2023 Western U.S. Event to describe a UAP that appeared partially transparent — celestial objects (stars) and terrain features could be seen *through* it. The object was associated with faint lights in a fixed formation (one round, one square) and frequently accompanied by a "cobweb" or "spider web" looking material in its vicinity.

## Characteristics

- **Translucency / partial transparency**: Stars, ridge crests, and vegetation were observed through the object. Described by Witness 1 as "like looking at something through water where it was not completely clear."
- **Shape**: Kite-like or flat horizontal line orientation. When lights were off, the object's shape and borders were "visually hard to define."
- **Light configuration**: One round light and one square light — observed on multiple occasions by different witnesses. When active, the round light appeared red, the square light appeared white.
- **Size**: Approx. 4 ft wide in one observation (horizontal line orientation). Object observed floating at 15-20 ft height.
- **Associated material**: "Dust and thick spider web looking material" or "cobweb" material observed floating in vicinity. Some moved with natural flow (descending); other material moved *upward* "not with a natural flow."
- **Behavior**: Floating, hovering with zero resistance. Evasive maneuvering described — "moving up and slightly side to side" giving "perception of trying to evade being noticed, or blend into the stars."
- **Silent**: No acoustics reported during any translucent kite encounter.

## Documented Examples

- **Witness 1** ([[summaries/dow-uap-d079-narrative-1-western-us-event]]): Observed translucent kite after vehicle-mimicry encounter. Object at 15-20 ft height with identifiable positions for two lights. Stars and terrain visible through object.
- **Witness 5** ([[summaries/dow-uap-d083-narrative-5-western-us-event]]): Observed identical cobweb material, one moving naturally downward, another moving upward unnaturally. Saw "faint lights in a perfect formation that moved together" within/through the material — formation appeared at "opposite angle" after eye readjustment. Evasive maneuvering noted.
- **Notional map** ([[summaries/dow-uap-d078-notional-map-western-us-event]]): Depicted as both "Dark Kite" (thin/dark kite-shaped object with one red + one white light) and "Translucent Kite" (translucent kite-shaped object at close range).
