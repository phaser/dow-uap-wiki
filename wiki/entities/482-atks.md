## Overview

The **482nd Attack Squadron (482 ATKS)** is a U.S. Air Force MQ-9 Reaper squadron that, in mid-to-late 2020, generated **six UAP-relevant Mission Reports** in the Persian Gulf / Strait of Hormuz / Iran AOR — every sortie logging at least one UAP and 1–5 Iranian Air Defense guard calls. The cluster supports NAVCENT under [[entities/centcom]], with tasking via [[entities/afcent]].

## Role / Activity

- Six MQ-9 Reaper sorties spanning **July, August (×2), September, October, and November 2020** — at the peak of post-Soleimani-strike U.S.-Iran tension.
- Persistent ISR presence over the Persian Gulf / Strait of Hormuz with extended on-station times (multi-hour orbits) — favorable conditions for capturing transient UAP events.
- The cluster is the strongest unit-level signal in the entire DoW UAP release set (`dow-uap-d<N>`).
