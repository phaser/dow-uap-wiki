## Overview

**Air Forces Central (AFCENT)** is the air component command of [[entities/centcom]], responsible for U.S. Air Force operations across the CENTCOM AOR. AFCENT-tasked ISR aircraft (likely MQ-9 Reaper, RC-135, U-2, or similar — types are redacted in released documents) are the originating platforms for several DoW UAP Mission Reports.

## Role / Activity

- Owns the MAJCOM-level tasking authority for ISR missions in the Middle East.
- Named explicitly as MAJCOM in [[summaries/dow-uap-d3-mission-report-arabian-gulf-2020]] (Misrep 8799515).
