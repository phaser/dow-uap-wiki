## Overview

**Air Materiel Command (AMC)** at [[entities/wright-patterson-afb]] was the U.S. Army Air Forces / Air Force command responsible for materiel acquisition, technical intelligence, and engineering analysis. Its **T-2 (Technical Intelligence) Division** ran the 1947–1949 UFO investigation under [[entities/project-sign]]. AMC's institutional gravity is the reason CONUS UFO reports flowed to Dayton, Ohio rather than to operational COCOMs.

## Role / Activity

- Hosted Project SIGN, Project GRUDGE, and (later) Project Blue Book under successor commands.
- Issued and maintained the [[concepts/incident-checklist-template]] form.
- Senior officers in the corpus include Maj. Gen. Charles P. Cabell, Col. H.M. McCoy (T-2), Lt. Col. George D. Garrett.
