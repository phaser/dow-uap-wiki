## Overview

Dr. Anthony O. Mirarchi was the Project Engineer for Project Twinkle, the Air Force's scientific study of green fireballs and unexplained aerial phenomena over New Mexico, conducted in collaboration with Land-Air, Inc. of Alamogordo.

## Role / Activity

The FBI Albuquerque Office contacted Mirarchi and briefed him on the Bureau's espionage and sabotage jurisdiction. Arrangements were made so that the FBI would be promptly advised if any information relative to these phenomena indicated any jurisdiction on the part of the Bureau. Mirarchi's contact with the FBI represents the intersection of the scientific investigation (Project Twinkle) and the FBI's national security concerns about the green fireball phenomenon near sensitive installations.
