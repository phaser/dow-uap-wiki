## Overview

**Apollo 12** (14–24 November 1969) was the second crewed lunar landing. Crew: Pete Conrad (CDR), Alan Bean (LMP), Richard Gordon (CMP). UAP-relevant content in the released transcript is thin: Bean's report of "particles escaping the Moon" through the Alignment Optical Telescope (AOT) — likely water-boiler vent particulates — and the usual tracking-light vs. co-moving-debris discrimination problem. The disclosure context is more important than any specific Apollo 12 sighting.

## Role / Activity

- Released as part of the NASA UAP disclosure document set (D1).
- No dramatic UAP event recorded — its inclusion appears to be precautionary / completionist.
