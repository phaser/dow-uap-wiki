## Overview

Borderland Sciences Research Associates (BSRA) was a San Diego, California UFO organization associated with Meade Layne (Director) and Max Freedom Long (Director). Members claimed to have predicted earthquakes and tidal waves, and to have been "in conversation with the men operating the flying saucers."

## Characteristics

- Directors: Meade Layne and Max Freedom Long
- Member Francis Ohm operated the Businessmens Assurance Company in San Diego, in the same building housing the FBI office
- Ohm claimed BSRA predicted the Tehachapi earthquake
- Predicted a Pacific tidal wave that would wash away Japanese islands, Hawaii, and the U.S. West Coast
- Predicted a new Pacific island
- Claimed flying saucers are "factual and actual" and on a "peaceful mission"
- Claimed saucers have "disintegrators" that will "disintegrate these planes completely in no time flat" if the U.S. continues to fire on them
- FBI SAC San Diego described Ohm as "a perfectly sane, sound individual" operating "a very large insurance business"
