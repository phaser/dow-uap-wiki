## Overview

**Major General Charles P. Cabell**, USAF, served as Director of Intelligence (AFOIN) and was the senior signing authority on the **AFOIN Memorandum No. 4, "Unconventional Aircraft," dated 15 February 1949** — the foundational doctrinal document that defined the Air Force's institutional posture on UFO reports as the [[entities/project-sign]] / [[entities/project-grudge]] transition was underway. The full text is preserved in **Serial 164 of the FBI HQ file [[entities/62-hq-83894]]**.

## Role / Activity

- Author/signer of AFOIN Memorandum No. 4 (1949) — see [[concepts/unconventional-aircraft]].
- Senior intelligence officer routing UFO reporting between AMC (Wright-Patterson) and AFOIN (HQ USAF).
- Cabell later became Deputy Director of CIA (1953–1962); his earlier UFO-relevant work in 1949 sits at a key institutional inflection point.
