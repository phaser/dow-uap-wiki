## Overview

**Clinton County Army Air Field** at Wilmington, Ohio, is the site of the multi-witness sighting cluster of **7 January 1948** (~1920–1955 EST), Incidents 48, 48a, 48b, 48c, 48d in the Project SIGN Box 7 incident-summary file. The cluster occurred on the **same evening as the [[entities/mantell-incident]]** at Godman AFB / Fort Knox, KY, and is cross-referenced with Incidents 30, 32, 33, 33a–33f as part of a multi-state event sequence.

## Role / Activity

Witnessed by Cpl. James H. Hudson (Control Tower), S/Sgt. John P. Haag, and Mr. Ralph L. Stirr. Object characteristics:

- Triangle / cone shape, SW of the field
- 15 000–20 000 ft altitude
- Color cycling **white → red → green**
- Surrounded by "burning gas" with green exhaust mist
- Ascending and descending vertically without forward motion
- 35 minutes total in sight, then off SW at high speed at ~1955 EST
