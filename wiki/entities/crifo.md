## Overview

Civilian Research, Interplanetary Flying Objects (CRIFO) was a UFO research organization founded and directed by L. H. Stringfield of Cincinnati, Ohio. It published the monthly CRIFO Newsletter with a claimed world-wide circulation of ~11,000 copies at $2/year.

## Characteristics

- Director: L. H. Stringfield (7017 Britton Avenue, Cincinnati 27, Ohio)
- Assisted by Stringfield's wife
- Monthly multi-lithographed Newsletter
- World-wide circulation: ~11,000 copies; subscription $2.00/year
- Stringfield communicated regularly with Frank Edwards (former AF of L radio news commentator) on the subject of saucers
- Edwards reportedly warned Stringfield to be careful or the Air Force would stop his publishing activities
- Stringfield claimed the Air Force was responsible for Edwards being fired from his AF of L radio program because Edwards used it to publicize his saucer interest
- Stringfield's Newsletter contained the "exclusive" Lt. Col. O'Mara interview claiming "Flying saucers 'do exist'"
