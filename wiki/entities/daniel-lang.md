## Overview

Daniel Lang was a writer who published a September 6, 1952 article in *The New Yorker* about Project Saucer. The article contained inaccurate claims that the FBI conducted investigations of flying saucers at the request of the Air Force.

## Role / Activity

The FBI conducted a counter-intelligence investigation of Lang's article. Verification with OSI, Air Technical Intelligence Center (Captain Ruppelt), and the Department of Defense (Mr. Albert Chop) established:
- The FBI has never been called upon to furnish reports on flying saucers
- Lang gathered most of his material ~2 years ago from newspaper sources
- Chop gave Lang "some routine items of interest" but instructed him not to contact ATIC
- At no time was the FBI mentioned by Chop
- Ruppelt believed Lang "made the story up or picked it up from some magazine or newspaper article sometime back"
