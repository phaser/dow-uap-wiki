## Overview

Donald Keyhoe was a UFO author best known for his book "The Flying Saucers are Real" (published 1950). The FBI's Crime Records Section reviewed the book and noted it contained false references to the FBI investigating flying saucers.

## Characteristics

- Wrote the article on saucers for *Liberty* magazine (referenced in the Louisville Adous letter)
- Book claimed FBI agents witnessed saucers at Las Vegas, New Mexico, 12-8-48
- Hoover's office noted the book "contains no quotations from the FBI but merely mentions this agency"

## Role / Activity

Keyhoe's book was reviewed by the FBI's Crime Records Section. The review noted that Keyhoe's claims about FBI involvement were not substantiated by the Bureau's records. The book is one of the most-cited early-UFO literature works and helped establish the extraterrestrial hypothesis in popular culture.
