## Overview

**DoW UAP** is the bureaucratic label applied to a set of declassified U.S. Department of War (formerly Department of Defense, renamed 2025) UAP documents released to the public via [[entities/aaro]]. The set is indexed with D-numbers (e.g., D3, D38, D49, D75) and contains Mission Reports (Misreps), range-fouler debriefs, email correspondence, and one or more launch-summary type reports. Geographic clustering is heavy: Middle East / Arabian Gulf / Strait of Hormuz / Persian Gulf and Western Pacific / Japan / East China Sea, with smaller clusters in Greece, the UAE, and CONUS.

## Role / Activity

- Source of ~41 declassified post-2016 UAP records in this corpus (D3–D75).
- Each Misrep follows a standard template: header (mission ID, MAJCOM, COCOM, service tasked, date/time, region, classification), narrative, UAP block (capabilities, propulsion, signatures, intelligent control), POC / QC / Approver block.
- Heavy redaction throughout — callsigns, units, tail numbers, basing under (b)(1)1.4a (national-defense classified info).
