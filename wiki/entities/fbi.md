## Overview

The U.S. Federal Bureau of Investigation has investigated unidentified flying objects since the 1947 wave, principally through HQ file **62-HQ-83894** (the "Flying Discs" / UFO file — see [[entities/62-hq-83894]]) and field-office files referenced via the 100-DE- and similar prefixes. The Bureau's interest in UAP has historically been at the periphery of its mission — espionage / domestic security implications of unexplained surveillance — but the released photographic and serial corpus in this wiki shows the Bureau accumulated a non-trivial operational record from 1947 onward.

## Role / Activity

- **Investigative collection** — field-office reports filed under file numbers like 100-DE-18221 (Denver field office) and 100-DE-26505. See [[summaries/100-de-18221-serial-844]] and [[summaries/100-de-26505]].
- **HQ-level UFO file** — 62-HQ-83894 collects nationwide flying-disc reports, correspondence with [[entities/j-edgar-hoover]], and field reports. See [[entities/62-hq-83894]].
- **Imagery** — the FBI released a 24-frame tracking-camera sequence from 1999-12-31 (timestamp may be a Y2K-era default) covering an ~11-minute electro-optical observation. See [[entities/fbi-tracking-camera-sequence-19991231]].
- **2020s Investigative Activity** — The FBI has conducted active UAP investigations in the 2020s, including: Colorado Springs Fort Carson incident (2022), documented in FD-302 and FD-1057 forms with forensic sketch production; Northeastern US recurrent orb sightings (2021–2024), involving site surveys, sensor data collection, and direct agent observations; and Western US Event digital forensic renderings (2023). See FBI-UAP D001–D023 series.
- **Post-2020 Production of Forensic Renderings** — The FBI Operational Projects Unit (OPU) produced forensic artist sketches/digital renderings for both the Colorado Springs 2022 incident and the 2023 Western US Event incidents, demonstrating a modern institutional capability for witness-guided visual reconstruction of UAP encounters.
- **Coordination with other agencies** — the FBI's UFO files frequently reference Air Force AMC / Project SIGN / [[entities/wright-patterson-afb]] cross-routing.
