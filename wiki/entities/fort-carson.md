## Overview

Fort Carson is a U.S. Army installation in Colorado Springs, Colorado, located near Cheyenne Mountain and the Cheyenne Mountain Complex (home to NORAD operations). In the UAP record, Fort Carson appears as the station from which five U.S. Army service members observed an airborne object over Cheyenne Mountain on 15 February 2022.

## Role / Activity

- Witness platform for the 2022 Cheyenne Mountain observation — five Army personnel stationed at Fort Carson reported the sighting that became [[entities/aaro|AARO]] case ICA-UAP-D001.
