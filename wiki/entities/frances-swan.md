## Overview

Frances Swan was a middle-class woman from South Berwick, Maine, who claimed to receive telepathic messages from "outer space" beginning May 27, 1954. She would write down messages for four or five hours at a time without getting tired. She was investigated by the FBI through Navy contacts.

## Characteristics

- Described by FBI Security Officer John Hutson as "very simple minded person, religious, a student of the Bible, and one who has studied spiritualism and other thought control systems"
- Had no more than a high school education, if that
- Received messages on a schedule: 6:00 AM, 12 noon, 6:00 PM daily
- Got a buzzing sound in her left ear when "on the line" — complained it was "very annoying and painful"
- Messages related to flying saucers, their location and purpose, life on other planets, life in the hereafter, and Bible prophesies
- Claimed contact with two space ships: M-4 (from Uranus, Commander "Affa") and L-11 (from Hatann, Commander "Ponnar")
- Ships described as 150 miles wide, 200 miles long, 100 miles deep; ~5,000 mother ships total (150–200 feet long)
- Contact purpose: protecting Earth from atomic/hydrogen bomb destruction which "disrupts the magnetic field of force"; also protecting the universe by repairing "fault lines" in the Pacific Ocean
- Husband Guy Swan and daughter Dawlyn heard buzzing but could not receive transmissions
- Said people from "outer space" could use her eyes and ears to see and hear via a "mechanical device"
- Claimed 5,000 "bells" or "flying saucers" would appear over many nations during August 1954 if physical contact were to be made
