## Overview

Frank Scully was the author of the book "Behind the Flying Saucers" and a subject of FBI investigation in 1950. Hoover ordered the Los Angeles SAC to determine whether Scully had been "actively engaged in communist activities since the late 1930s."

## Role / Activity

In October 1950, Hoover sent a teletype to the LA SAC instructing them to "DISCREETLY DETERMINE THROUGH APPROPRIATE RELIABLE SOURCES" whether Frank Scully, author of "Behind the Flying Saucers," was the same person who had been "actively engaged in communist activities since the late 1930s in the territory of your office." A follow-up teletype on October 18 ordered "IMMEDIATELY RESULTS OF YOUR INVESTIGATION TO IDENTIFY FRANK SCULLY." The result of this investigation is not preserved in section 6.
