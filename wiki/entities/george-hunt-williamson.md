## Overview

George Hunt Williamson described himself as an archeologist and writer associated with *Valor* magazine and Soulcraft Press in Noblesville, Indiana. He was a co-organizer of the planned Truman Bethurum Cincinnati lecture.

## Characteristics

- Associated with *Valor* magazine, subtitled "Golden Times Weekly," published by Soulcraft Press, P.O. Box 192, Noblesville, Indiana
- Soulcraft Press was operated by William Dudley Pelley at Soul Craft Chapels
- *Valor* dealt with "practical mysticism, the spiritual movement called Soulcraft and the relationship between earth man and the individuals arriving on this planet from flying saucers"
- Claimed these individuals arriving in flying saucers are "of greater intelligence and learning than the earth people and are here to give the earth people intellectual guidance and spiritual endowment and guidance"
- Co-organized Bethurum's Taft Auditorium lecture (cancelled) with Henry Maday as ticket agent
