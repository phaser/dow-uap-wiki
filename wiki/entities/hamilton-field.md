## Overview

**Hamilton Field** in California is the site of **Incident 8** of [[entities/project-sign]] — 29 July 1947, 1450 local — Capt. William H. Ryherd (Asst. Operations Officer, USAF) and Lt. Ward Stewart observed two white, shiny circular objects 15–25 ft diameter at very high speed above a P-80, flying south toward Oakland.

## Role / Activity

- Witnessed by an Asst. Ops officer and a serving lieutenant — high-credibility witness chain.
- Incident 8 is also the source of an inferred concept page on [[concepts/formation-flight]] for the early-wave era.
- A related Hamilton Field-area case (Incident 58, ~1948) appears partially OCR-corrupt in the sample of [[summaries/army-incident-summaries-1-100]] but describes a milk-white object behaving "similar to fighter a/c when accompanying heavier ships."
