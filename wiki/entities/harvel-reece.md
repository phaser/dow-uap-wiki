## Overview

Harvel F. Reece was a citizen who wrote to the FBI in October 1952 regarding flying-saucer observations. His letter was forwarded to the Air Force OSI.

## Role / Activity

Reece's letter dated October 21, 1952, was received by the Bureau and forwarded to the Director of Special Investigations, Inspector General, Department of the Air Force. Reece was advised that his letter had been referred to the Air Force. No further action was taken by the Bureau.
