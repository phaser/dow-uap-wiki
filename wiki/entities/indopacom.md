## Overview

**U.S. Indo-Pacific Command (INDOPACOM)** is the unified COCOM responsible for U.S. military operations across the Indo-Pacific theater (Japan, Korea, Western Pacific, East China Sea, etc.). In the DoW UAP release set, INDOPACOM is the second-largest geographic source after [[entities/centcom]], with cases including the East China Sea (2024), Japan (2023), and Pacific time-zone email correspondence threads.

## Role / Activity

- COCOM of record for Western Pacific UAP Mission Reports.
- Subject of email correspondence release D50 (April 2025).
