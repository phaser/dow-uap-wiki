## Overview

The International Flying Saucer Bureau (IFSB) was a UFO organization founded by Albert K. Bender of Bridgeport, Connecticut. It published the house organ *Space Review* and had international branches including a British branch in Bristol.

## Characteristics

- President & Editor: Albert K. Bender (Bridgeport CT)
- VP & Treasurer: Max Krengel
- Secretary: Allan C. Rievman
- British branch in Bristol
- Appointed local representatives nationwide (e.g., Robert D. Wolf in Franklin IN)
- Collected and disseminated international saucer reports
- Published "Theory No. 1-10" by IFSB members offering ET hypotheses
- Dissolved in 1953 after Bender's "three men in black" experience
