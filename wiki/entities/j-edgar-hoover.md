## Overview

**J. Edgar Hoover**, Director of the [[entities/fbi]] from 1924 to 1972, is the primary correspondent on the FBI flying-disc file [[entities/62-hq-83894]] from its 1947 origin through his retirement. The famous Guy Hottel memo (Washington Field Office, 22 March 1950) was addressed to Hoover and forms one of the better-known artifacts of FBI UFO involvement.

## Role / Activity

- Approving / routing authority for FBI engagement with flying-disc cases.
- Recipient of field-office reports and inter-agency cables on the UFO subject through the 1950s and into the 1960s.
