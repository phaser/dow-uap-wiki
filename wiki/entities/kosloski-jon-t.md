## Overview

Jon T. Kosloski is the Director of the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO), the Department of War office responsible for investigating Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP). He authored or signed all AARO memoranda regarding the October 2023 Western U.S. Event, appearing as the cognizant authority on each witness narrative memo and the unresolved case analysis update.

## Role / Activity

- **Director, AARO** — Signatory on six AARO memoranda for record dated 02 June 2026 and the analytical memorandum dated 05 June 2026
- Oversees AARO's analytic tradecraft for UAP case resolution, including cross-correlation of witness narratives against flight logs, radar data, ADS-B data, and Blue/Red Force deconfliction
- Responsible for AARO's stated intent to integrate scientific modeling capabilities from partner agencies, expertise from scientific/academic institutions, and multi-domain multispectral data exploitation
- Directed the collection of free-form narratives from the six reporting agents, preserving testimony "as provided by first-hand observers"
