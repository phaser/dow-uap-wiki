## Overview

Michael Halfery was a New Orleans, Louisiana individual who sold photographs depicting flying saucers and a "man from Mars" to a third party in May 1950.

## Characteristics

- Address: 2453 Urquhart Street, New Orleans, Louisiana
- Sold photographs for $1.00 to John R. Esposito (615 Piety Street, New Orleans)
- Esposito gave the photographs to CID, New Orleans Port of Embarkation

## Role / Activity

On May 22, 1950, Halfery sold two photographs to Esposito for $1.00. The photographs depicted flying saucers and a man from Mars in the custody of two U.S. Army Military Policemen. Esposito turned them over to CID, which forwarded copies to the FBI. Intelligence Branch classified the source as "usually reliable" and the information as "probably true."
