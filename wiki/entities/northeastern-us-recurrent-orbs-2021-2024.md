## Overview

A **multi-year recurrent UAP pattern** at a [REDACTED] residence in the Northeastern United States, spanning November 2021 through at least December 2024. A single civilian witness (identity redacted) reported hundreds of observations of anomalous luminous phenomena — "plasma orbs" in FBI terminology — near their property. The FBI conducted a multi-phase investigation including: witness interviews, collection of video/photo evidence, two site surveys, and a surveillance visit during which **FBI Special Agents themselves directly observed anomalous light phenomena**. The case is notable for the reported correlation between sightings and gamma radiation spikes, electronic/device anomalies, trail camera documentation, and the neighbor corroboration.

## Role / Activity

- **Witness**: [REDACTED] — lived at residence since 2006; had no prior interest in UAP and described self as "a skeptic" before November 2021
- **First observation**: November 2021 at ~5:00 AM — singular light that split into 2–3 lights forming a triangular pattern with a center light; disappeared after accidental flash photo
- **Subsequent observations**: Hundreds of sightings over three years; objects seen as close as 200 yards; behaviors include hovering, lateral movement, morphing; observed above horizon, within trees, and near water
- **Sensor deployment**: Trail cameras and other sensors deployed; witness reported gamma radiation spikes correlating with sighting times; electronic/GPS anomalies during encounters
- **Corroboration**: Neighbor to the south confirmed seeing strange lights in the tree line
- **LinkedIn contact**: A contact told the witness "people have been hurt by these things" and shared a personal 1996 New Mexico vehicle-shutdown incident (Fort Bliss to Fort Carson)
- **FBI investigation timeline**:
  - August 2024: Witness referred to FBI by liaison
  - October 2024: Initial FBI contact; videos and data provided
  - October 2024: In-person FBI interview
  - November 2024: FBI site survey (D006) — wooded area ~200 yards behind home, trail camera, stone wall, restricted area signage
  - November 2024: FBI surveillance visit (D007) — **two FBI Special Agents directly observed anomalous light phenomena** over ~1.5 hours, including white pulsing lights at treeline, white/blue flashes, broad light plumes, and a paired white-over-red light at the pond that exhibited instantaneous re-appearance
  - December 2024: Follow-up site survey (D008) — agents assessed nighttime drone operation impractical due to tree cover; no ground-level infrastructure could explain phenomena
  - Witness offered property for future equipment deployment and coordinated evening visits
