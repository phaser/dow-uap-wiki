## Overview

On **7 July 1947** in **Phoenix, Arizona**, **William Albert Rhodes** photographed a heel-shaped object in flight — among the earliest photographic evidence captured during the [[syntheses/1947-flying-disc-wave]]. The negatives were collected by the Air Force / FBI; the negative-handover correspondence is preserved in **Section 2 of the FBI HQ file [[entities/62-hq-83894]]**.

## Role / Activity

- One of the very first photographic UFO cases of the modern era.
- The negatives' handover chain (Rhodes → AFB → FBI) is itself documented, providing rare provenance traceability for early photographic evidence.
