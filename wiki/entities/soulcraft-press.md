## Overview

Soulcraft Press was a publishing operation at Noblesville, Indiana, operated by William Dudley Pelley. It published *Valor* magazine and various pamphlets on practical mysticism and the Soulcraft spiritual movement.

## Characteristics

- Location: Noblesville, Indiana (Soul Craft Chapels)
- Operator: William Dudley Pelley
- Published *Valor* magazine ("Golden Times Weekly")
- Published pamphlets on practical mysticism and the relationship between earth people and "individuals arriving on this planet from flying saucers"
- Claimed these saucer-arriving individuals are "of greater intelligence and learning than the earth people and are here to give the earth people intellectual guidance and spiritual endowment and guidance"
