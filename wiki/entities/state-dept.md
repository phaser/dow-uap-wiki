## Overview

The **U.S. Department of State** appears in this corpus as the originating or transit agency for diplomatic cables and embassy reports of UAPs from foreign jurisdictions. State's UAP record is institutionally subordinate to the operational UAP record (DoD/DoW, FBI), but the State cables provide an under-appreciated cross-cultural and foreign-corroboration layer — particularly when local foreign-press, foreign-military, or commercial-aviation accounts are routed back to Washington via embassy channels.

## Role / Activity

- **Embassy cable reporting** — Moscow (Ambassador Vershbow on Russian UFOs and Georgia overflights, 2001); Ashgabat (Turkmenistan UFOlogist NGO, 2004); Mexico City (Mexican Congress UAP hearing, September 2023, with Maussan and Ryan Graves); Port Moresby (Papua New Guinea, January 1985 Air Niugini overflights); Dushanbe (Kazakhstan, January 1994, Tajik Air 747SP Capt. Ed Rhodes at 41 000 ft).
- **Inter-agency policy correspondence** — the **Maxwell W. Hunter II 1963 memo** to Robert F. Packard at State, "Thoughts on the Space Alien Race Question," is preserved in this corpus and is one of the earliest formal U.S. policy memos addressing first-contact contingencies. See [[entities/hunter-policy-memo-1963]].
- **COMETA dissemination** — Carol Rosin's 2001 cover letter forwarding the [[entities/cometa-report]] (1999) to NASA was routed via State channels.
