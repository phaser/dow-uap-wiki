## Overview

Truman Bethurum was a 55-year-old former truck driver from Redondo Beach, California, who became one of the first self-described "contactees" — individuals claiming to have personally interacted with beings from flying saucers.

## Role / Activity

Bethurum claimed in the *Daily Breeze* (31 December 1953) that he had been aboard flying saucers eleven times and met "a ravishing woman commandant" of a saucer crew in the Nevada desert. He advertised a lecture at Taft Auditorium in Cincinnati (June 11, 1954) on "the real flying saucer story" via George Hunt Williamson's *Valor* magazine. The Cincinnati lecture was cancelled after a disagreement over ticket sales. Bethurum returned to California; a fall 1954 Cincinnati return was possible. His story was published in *Valor* magazine (Soulcraft Press, Noblesville IN), with Bethurum's own handwritten statement: "This is a true story, a factual experience."
