## Overview

*Valor* magazine was a weekly publication subtitled "Golden Times Weekly," published by Soulcraft Press at P.O. Box 192, Noblesville, Indiana. It dealt with practical mysticism, the Soulcraft spiritual movement, and the relationship between earth people and "individuals arriving on this planet from flying saucers."

## Characteristics

- Publisher: William Dudley Pelley (Soulcraft Press)
- Editors: George Hunt Williamson and others
- Content included articles on Truman Bethurum and his saucer encounters
- Featured Bethurum's *Daily Breeze* (Dec 31, 1953) article about meeting "a ravishing woman commandant" in the Nevada desert
- Bethurum's handwritten certification on the article: "This is a true story, a factual experience"
- Issues 15 and 16 of volume 6 (Feb 6 and Feb 13, 1954) were cited by the FBI
