## Overview

A **2025 multi-witness UAP event** in the Western United States in which a helicopter crew (**senior IC official as Witness 1**, plus federal-LE agents designated USPER1 through USPER7) observed a low-altitude orb *within 10 feet* of their aircraft, plus larger formations of orbs displaying behaviors that include sequential flare-up patterns ("**[[concepts/orbs-launching-orbs]]**"), apparent target switching from the helicopter to passing military training aircraft, and at least one **"Fiery Orb"** estimated at 12–18 m diameter at 1 050 m altitude. Visual signatures across the witness corpus include "**Dark Kite**" (later assessed as a triangular craft), a "**Transparent Kite**" (NVG-partial-transparent, light-beam truncation), and the recurring small-orb form factor.

## Role / Activity

- One of the most kinematically dramatic recent events in the released U.S. UAP record — orbs traversing within helicopter rotor distance, flare-up formation behaviour, NVG-only / partial-transparent imaging.
- Witness chain is a senior IC official plus 6+ trained federal LE agents — high-credibility witness corpus per [[concepts/witness-reliability-evaluation]].
- The slide deck (`western_us_event_slides_5.08.2026.pdf`) catalogues the event with annotated frames; the redacted USPER statement is the textual primary record.
- The event is referenced in 2025–2026 Congressional UAP discourse but the operational underlying file remains classified.
