## Overview

William Dudley Pelley operated Soulcraft Press at Noblesville, Indiana, which published the weekly journal *Valor*. Pelley was the onetime founder of the fascist Silver Legion (a U.S. syndicalist movement).

## Characteristics

- Operated Soul Craft Chapels at Noblesville, Indiana
- Published *Valor* magazine, subtitled "Golden Times Weekly," described as "a journal of the applied spirituality"
- *Valor* dealt with "practical mysticism, the spiritual movement called Soulcraft and the relationship between earth man and the individuals arriving on this planet from flying saucers"
- Previously known as founder of the Silver Legion, a U.S. fascist organization; his history was cited by the FBI as part of the background on the Bethurum/Williamson operation
