## Overview

**Wright-Patterson Air Force Base** (Dayton, Ohio) is the home of [[entities/air-materiel-command]] (AMC) and the historical center of U.S. Air Force technical intelligence. It hosted [[entities/project-sign]], [[entities/project-grudge]], and Project Blue Book. By default, every UAP-relevant report from a CONUS AAF/USAF unit in the 1947–1969 era was routed to Wright-Patterson for AMC T-2 (Technical Intelligence) analysis.

## Role / Activity

- Headquarters of the U.S. UFO investigation effort 1947–1969.
- Cited in case correspondence (e.g., the Strapp/Muroc Incident 4 names *"Mr. Lens (civilian, Wright Field, Dayton, OH)"* as a witness, confirming the investigator base of operations).
- Repository for the original Incident Summary check-list files (NARA RG 38, container 143685, Box 7) before NARA transfer.
