## Key Points

- Section 8 is dominated by the FBI's reluctant brushes with the **contactee movement** — the new (post-1952) genre of self-described flying-saucer experiencers like **George Adamski**, **Truman Bethurum**, **George Hunt Williamson**, and **Daniel Fry**, plus the unique case of **Mrs. Frances Swan**.
- **Truman Bethurum / George Hunt Williamson, Cincinnati OH, June 1954** — Thomas Eickhoff (3721 Tappan Avenue, operator of Tom's Beauty Salon) walks into FBI Cincinnati on 7 June 1954 reporting that:
  - Henry Maday (364 W. Lewiston Ave, Ferndale MI) had advertised in the *Cincinnati Enquirer* a paid lecture by Truman Bethurum at Taft Auditorium ($2/ticket) on "the real flying saucer story."
  - Bethurum (Redondo Beach CA, age 55, former truck driver) claimed in the *Daily Breeze* (31 December 1953) that he had been aboard flying saucers eleven times and met "a ravishing woman commandant" of a saucer crew in the Nevada desert.
  - Williamson (associated with *Valor* magazine, Soulcraft Press, Noblesville IN, run by **William Dudley Pelley** — onetime fascist Silver Legion founder) describes himself as an archeologist and writer.
  - Eickhoff visited Lt. Col. John O'Mara at Wright-Patterson AFB on 6 June 1954 and was told by O'Mara that the Air Force has no flying saucers, and that **Major Donald Keyhoe**, retired USMC and author of *Flying Saucers from Outer Space*, "is a fraud" — a statement Eickhoff later relayed to Keyhoe himself, who "was going to Washington with his attorneys" to take it up with the Air Force.
  - Eickhoff intends to support a Bethurum lecture in Cincinnati and report to Federal authorities; planned a 7 June 1954 dinner at the home of **L. H. Stringfield** (7017 Britton Ave Cincinnati 27), with Williamson, Manspeaker, Bethurum, Ralph Zimmermann, and Eickhoff. Stringfield is a young UFO researcher who would later author *Situation Red: The UFO Siege!* and the "crash retrieval" Status Reports.
- **Mrs. Frances Swan / Adm. Herbert B. Knowles case (29 July 1954)** — John Hutson (Security Officer, Bureau of Aeronautics, US Navy) and Cdr. L. T. McQuiston interview the FBI WFO. Retired Rear Admiral Herbert B. Knowles of South Berwick ME — Mrs. Swan's neighbor — wrote to Adm. Espey, Director of Naval Intelligence, on 1 June 1954 enclosing telepathic messages received by Swan from "outer space"; she would write down messages "for four or five hours at a time without getting tired." ONI declined to act in absence of evidence of conversations with foreign powers; Bureau of Aeronautics also declines.
- **Carl R. Keyser sighting (Milford OH, 23 July 1954, 14:40)** — Civil Defense Col. J. M. Smith reports to FBI Cincinnati that Keyser and his wife observed a silver circular/spherical object 45° SE of their residence on Route 1 Milford. Referred to OSI Wright-Patterson.
- **Charles A. Yost / OC Research Laboratory letter (St. Clair Shores MI, 14 November 1953)** — letter to "Mr. Small" (a typesetter for the Nome Nugget in Alaska) found by Dr. Kennidy of Nome and forwarded by Major William H. Greenhalgh, Jr., 5004th Air Intelligence Service Squadron, to OSI Alaskan Air Command, then to FBI HQ. Yost says his lab's research is being kept secret, has a "connection with the flying saucer" but is "secondary" to research on **electric fields**, and references work by a "research foundation in Western United States" not connected to him. Letter is filed with no further action by FBI.
- **George Adamski reference**: Eickhoff mentions Adamski's *Flying Saucers Have Landed* (Werner-Lowery / British Book Center) in context of Bethurum credibility.
- **Donald Keyhoe reference**: *Flying Saucers from Outer Space* (Henry Holt / Harper's Publishers) cited as source-text Eickhoff was reading; O'Mara's "Keyhoe is a fraud" comment becomes the most quotable line in the section.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **December 1953, Nevada desert** — Truman Bethurum's claimed contact with "Captain Aura Rhanes" of the planet Clarion (eleven encounters, per *Daily Breeze* 31 Dec 1953); FBI documents Bethurum's emerging career as a contactee.
- **23 July 1954, Milford OH** — Carl R. Keyser silver-spherical-object sighting.
- **23 July 1954 ff., South Berwick ME** — Mrs. Frances Swan automatic-writing "outer space" telepathy case, surfaced by Adm. Knowles to ONI.
