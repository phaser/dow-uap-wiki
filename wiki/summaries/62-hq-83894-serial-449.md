## Key Points

- Serial 449 is the FBI HQ retention copy of **AFSCA's *Flying Saucers International* magazine Issue No. 24, July 1966** — the same publication discussed in [[summaries/62-hq-83894-section-10]] as forwarded by Mrs. Florence C. Dow.
- The serial was opened because Mr. Jarvis H. Cooper (IRS, 401 N Broad St Philadelphia) — who had subscribed because his son was interested in flying saucers — believed the cover article on pages 2-3 "expounded the Communist Party (CP) line." The Philadelphia Division forwarded to LA on 22 September 1966.
- **Cover article**: a "channeled" message titled "BRIGHT WORLD IN HANDS OF SAUCER MOVEMENT," allegedly from **Master Kalen-Li Retan, head of the planet Korendor (about 400 light years distant)**, received 4 May 1966 via "special directional short-wave radio" by **Bob Renaud**, a young electronic-technician whose contactee story had run in *UFO International* issues #18-22.
- **Content of the Kalen-Li Retan message**:
  - Korendians have been in contact with Earth via Renaud since 1961; Renaud has visited their undersea and underground bases, been aboard their craft, even piloted scout craft.
  - Earth's condition is "appalling"; Vietnam war is "really" being run by the Pentagon and "Defense"-oriented industries (the **Military-Industrial Complex**), not the President or Congress.
  - JFK was assassinated for opposing the Military-Industrial Complex.
  - Vietnam war is being prolonged because peace would crash the US economy (Dow-Jones drop on a peace rumor cited as evidence).
  - Solution proposed: "Universal Economics" — a non-money system already practiced on advanced planets.
  - Korendians describe themselves: humanoid, indistinguishable from humans except naturally 3-4 ft tall, scaled proportionately, average tan-skin coloration ("NOT green, blue, or heliotrope"); they speak "Korendian" (related to Galingua) — note "Galingua" is presented as an interplanetary universal tongue from which Latin and English borrowed.
  - Beings have "infiltrated" the planet; they directly intervene in the United States, China, Russia, Vietnam.
- **Pasted-in event listings** show the era's contactee circuit: Dr. Frank E. Stranges' 4th Annual UFO Space and Science Convention (Blarney Castle Inn LA, Feb 5-6 1966); Dr. Daniel W. Fry's Man in Space Symposium (Dunsmuir, near Mt. Shasta, June 4-6 1966); Buck Nelson's 10th Annual Spacecraft Convention (Mountain View, MO, June 25-27 1966); AFSCA 3rd National Convention (Reno NV, July 8-10 1966).
- **Speaker list at AFSCA 3rd National Convention**: Wayne S. Aho, Carl A. Anderson, Orfeo Angelucci, Michael "X" Barton, Wesley Bateman, Marianne Francis, Dr. Daniel W. Fry, Calvin C. Girvin, Gabriel Green (editor), Dr. George King (Aetherius Society), Hanno Mayberry, Howard Menger, Laura Mundo, Mel Noel, Sidney Padrick, Chief Standing Horse, Dr. Frank E. Stranges, Mollie Thompson, Hope Troxell, George W. Van Tassel, Hal Wilcox.
- **AFSCA recommended reading list** preserved (a snapshot of mid-60s contactee canon):
  - *Council of Seven Lights* (George Van Tassel, $3.65)
  - *The Advent of the Cosmic Viewpoint* (Bryant Reeve, $6.15)
  - *Flying Saucers Through the Ages* (Paul Thomas, $4.15)
  - *Other Tongues, Other Flesh* (George Hunt Williamson, $6.10)
  - *Secret of the Andes* (Brother Philip)
  - *Flying Saucers and the Three Men* (Albert K. Bender)
  - *Flying Saucers — Serious Business* (Frank Edwards)
  - *Incident at Exeter* (John G. Fuller)
  - *The Hollow Earth* (Dr. Raymond Bernard)
- **FBI conclusion**: "The indices of the Los Angeles Division contain no information identifiable with Renaud, and no investigation has ever been conducted on captioned organization. No further action is contemplated."

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- The Bob Renaud / Korendor contact story (1961-1966) is one of the better-documented mid-60s "radio contactee" narratives, preserved in this Bureau retention.
- The **Mel Noel** circuit reference is notable: he claimed to have flown F-86 fighter pursuits of UFOs as an Air Force lieutenant.
