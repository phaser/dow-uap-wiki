## Key Points

- **Source**: Former Soviet citizen who served at the Sary Shagan weapons testing range in Kazakhstan.
- **Summary content**: Report provides limited information on Sary Shagan range facilities, work areas, security fencing, regional headquarters (V/Ch 03080), and a warhead checkout unit (V/Ch 03142).
- **System-75 (SA-2) warheads**: Site 4 checked warheads for the SA-2 surface-to-air missile.
- **System-300 / Aldan (ABM-1 GALOSH)**: Site 4 also checked warheads for ABM-1/GALOSH anti-ballistic missile.
- **Rumored laser research**: Experiments involving laser weapons reportedly conducted at an unknown location at the range.
- **Unidentified aerial phenomenon**: Source observed an unidentified bright green circular object or mass in the sky in late summer 1973. Green concentric circles formed around the mass within 10-15 seconds. No sound. Coloring disappeared within minutes.
