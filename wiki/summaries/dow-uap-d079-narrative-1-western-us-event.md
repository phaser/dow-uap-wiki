## Key Points

First-hand free-form narrative from Witness 1, one of six federal law enforcement special agents who reported observing UAP over two days in October 2023 in the western United States. Submitted in response to AARO's request.

### Night 1 / Night 2 Observations

- **Orange mother orb near hillside**: Witness 1 and partner driving through a valley noticed a large glowing ball to their right at 90 degrees. Estimated 500-600 meters away; later range-finder measurement to hillside was ~1,100 meters. Described as "a circle of light that looked like a swirly pattern of bright lava." Well-defined circle with no visible structure at edges. Through binoculars appeared to have "a line or spindle" below possibly touching the hillside. No glow on hillside or ground. Observed 1-2 minutes before disappearing (did not fade — just no longer visible). No sound. Estimated diameter: 20+ feet.
- **Orange orb launching red orbs**: ~20 minutes later, another similar object lit up over hillside to the front-right, "several miles away." Lasted 1-2 seconds. Expelled three red lights that "flew out of and away from it," evenly spaced, moving fast. Flash from over the horizon to the left accompanied this.
- **Repeat event**: ~20 minutes later, orange ball appeared front-left. Three red lights departed in a different pattern: one straight out, one up horizontally, one down horizontally — vertical alignment of three lights. Flash accompanied again.
- **Vehicle mimicry encounter**: Hours later while driving, observed a vehicle ahead with one red taillight and one white taillight (one round, one square). Vehicle moved off road as if hovering, did not bounce with terrain, produced no dust. Lights appeared to face them regardless of viewing angle. When they stopped, lights turned off and "a darker object" moved upward and behind them, flying away. No sound. "Reminded me of a Toyota Prius."
- **Translucent kite object**: 1-2 hours later, observed "dust and thick spider web looking material floating." Saw same object floating in more vertical orientation, in the air 15-20 feet up. Could see a star, crest of hill, and vegetation *through* the object. "Like looking at something through water where it was not completely clear." Could identify spots for the two lights (one round, one square), but lights were off. Shape and borders visually hard to define.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- Orange orb near hillside with possible spindle/structure below
- Orange orb launching three red orbs (two repetitions, different ejection patterns)
- Vehicle-mimicking object with anomalous lighting and hovering movement
- Translucent kite-shaped object observed through NV and naked eye, with visible light-position markings
