## Key Points

First-hand free-form narrative from Witness 2 describing three distinct incidents over the two-day October 2023 period. The witness provides the most detailed and analytically rich descriptions of any single narrative, including a "portal" analogy. AI-generated sketches accompany.

### Incident 1: Orbs Launching Orbs (Northern Horizon)

- **Time/Location**: ~1900 hours, valley with mountain ranges west/east, further ranges north. Clear weather, dusk.
- **Observation**: Bright circular orb-like light ~35-45 degrees above northern/northeastern horizon. Grew slowly brighter. Had a smaller red orb-like light *within* the bright orb.
- **Ejection**: The bright orb expelled three or four red lights that "accelerated instantly and maneuvered with perfect, smooth coordination into a horizontal formation." Red lights were ~1/15 the size of the host orb.
- **Appearance**: Bright orb had "a bright natural light with a slight halo effect" — *not* an artificial neon glow. Red lights appeared more like artificial lights (e.g., "taillights").
- **Repeat**: Process repeated ~5 times between ~1900-1930 across the northern, northeastern, and eastern horizon. Other teams confirmed.
- **Stationary red lights**: The last group of red lights moved in horizontal pattern to the southern horizon where they remained ~1900-2300 (3+ hours). Occasionally dipped below horizon but rose back up. No acoustics, interference, or physiological effects.

### Witness 2's Portal Analogy

> "It looked like a mix of mechanical deployment and biological division; it appeared too smooth and agile to be mechanical, but too structured/coordinated to be a biological division. To offer an obscure yet similar description, I find the characterization of portals oddly comparable to the event I witnessed. The bright orb-like light was small, grew larger, appeared to project objects out of it, and then it dimmed until it was no longer visible; not dissimilar to the opening and closing of a portal through which objects travel and deploy forward."

### Incident 2: Vehicle Mimicry in Valley

- Two vehicles with headlights flashing methodically in a ~6x6 mile box between mountain ranges. Red lights hovering ~15 degrees above southern horizon, appearing to cover "the area of a football field."
- Object "floated with an impossible, eerie stillness."
- When agents drove toward one vehicle, its headlights morphed into a single bright white/orange orb-like light. At ~50 yards, object accelerated instantly south covering ~1 mile in <1 minute.
- Object later appeared as "a group of orange and purple lights in the shape of a box (not dissimilar to a semi-truck)" that glided across rugged terrain covering ~4 miles in ~1 minute.
- Objects appeared to stay within 20 feet of ground. "Highly reactive to our interaction." "The object appeared to 'toy' with us."
- Triangle/red lights over horizon "reminiscent of the Phoenix lights event/incident in the 1990s."

### Incident 3: Road Orb and Semi-Truck Mimicry

- ~2100-2300 hours. Single orb-like light on rural highway ~1-2 miles away. They drove toward it at ~80 mph but could not catch up.
- Object maintained height of a vehicle above right lane, loosely following road. On several occasions "the bright orb-like light dimmed out and transitioned to what appeared to be a single red taillight but then transitioned back."
- Toward end, object "abruptly transitioned into an orange light/orb-like-disk with a halo glow" and glided to roadside.
- A semi-truck then came around the corner heading toward them at high speed. When they looked back, the orb-like-disk was gone. Unclear if semi-truck was the same object.
- "Other vehicles passed heading east and no one appeared to react to the object we were seeing." Video footage captured some of encounter; "more valuable is the audio of our reactions."
