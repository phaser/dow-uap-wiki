## Key Points

First-hand free-form narrative from Witness 3 describing two occurrences in mid-October 2023.

### Occurrence 1: Orange Orb Releasing Red Orbs

- **Time**: ~1900, roughly dusk, clear sky
- **Observation**: Large orange orb in the sky heading toward witness. Alternated between clear and orange in a ~2-3 second cycle. When orange, outline well-defined; when clear, outline still vaguely visible.
- **Movement**: Moved ~20 degrees in sky in 3-5 seconds. At ~45 degrees (halfway to overhead), expelled three smaller red orbs.
- **Ejection**: Described as appearing "mechanical." Red orbs moved south in a straight line spaced ~1 second apart, maintaining 45-degree trajectory until out of sight.
- **Scale comparison**: "The red orbs were like grapes being expelled from a basketball."
- **Duration**: ~30-45 seconds total. No sound, no exhaust trails.

### Occurrence 2: Red Lights Over Airfield / Vehicle Pursuit

- **Time**: Either same night or next night, ~2000-2030, near full darkness, clear sky
- **Observation**: Four red lights from the north moving south across ~90 degrees of sky in 5-10 seconds. Stopped above an airfield and hovered in a square formation over each corner.
- **Orange orbs**: Two orange orbs observed coming from east, hovering near slope of west-facing ridgeline while red lights hovered over airfield.
- **Vehicle interaction**: A vehicle below their elevated position was flashing headlights at a "slow, methodical tempo" (2-3 seconds between off/on). Agent and partner turned off their own lights and drove down using NVGs.
- **Pursuit**: Could not close within ~100 yards — "it seemed like the vehicle was responding to our approach." Vehicle then "seemingly floated off the road to the south, across rough terrain." No roads present, no tire tracks found afterward.
- **Disappearance**: Vehicle's single remaining light faded out. When vehicle was gone, all orbs also gone.
- **Duration**: ~2-3 minutes. No sound.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- Alternating clear/orange orb cycling — suggests partial transparency or variable luminosity
- "Mechanical" ejection of red orbs described with grape/basketball scale
- Square-formation hover of four red lights over airfield corners
- Synchronized disappearance of vehicle and all orbs simultaneously
