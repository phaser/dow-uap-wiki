## Key Points

First-hand free-form narrative from Witness 4 describing multi-night observations and unusual light formations.

### Night 1 Observations

- **Fast-moving light**: Shortly after sunset (~1900), bright ball of light like a shooting star crossing over a hillside. Approx. 1,000-2,000 feet above ground level. Orange/yellow/white. Lasted ~1-2 seconds. Color sequence: amber to orange to slightly yellow to white. No sound.
- **Multiple orange lights/orbs**: ~10 minutes later, saw multiple singular bright orange lights/orbs of various brightness, size, and distance. Through NVGs saw additional lights organized in a line of three, evenly spaced, at various altitudes horizontally above skyline. Most visible 1-2 seconds.
- **"Hatching" behavior**: Multiple very bright orange lights appeared to "shoot out smaller orange lights as if being hatched from the larger very bright orange light." Occurred 5+ times. Smaller lights traveled downward "like a falling flare."
- **Nine-light horizontal line**: Nine lights formed a horizontal line just above a ridgeline — static, evenly spaced, visible for 30+ seconds. Smaller lights appeared below and to left/right, clustered in groups of three (almost touching).
- **Reappearance**: Lights disappeared then reappeared "directly behind me."
- **Total duration**: 2-3 hour span, nonstop.

### Night 2 Observations

- Same light phenomena observed between 1900-2100. Additionally saw:
- **Chemtrails**: White trails ~10 finger-lengths from highest ridge point, observed >10 minutes. One chemtrail disappeared; the one below made a U-turn and headed toward the ground very quickly. A plane was observed in same direction.
- **Starlink-like deployment**: String of lights resembling a Starlink deployment.
- **Orb generating 4 lights**: Light source just above southeast ridgeline produced four smaller lights. Three formed a horizontal line, fourth traveled westward. Observed ~10 minutes.

### Mountain-Traversing Light

- A light "darted between the top and bottom of the mountain within seconds" — not feasible for a standard vehicle. Desert terrain, rough dirt roads, no dust cloud, moved steadily. Traversed mountain to mountain for ~30 minutes.
- "A very bright, white light" appeared just below a ridgeline, "steady and did not pulse or hatch smaller lights. At first, it looked like a star; however, it was big, very bright and looked like it was in front of and just below a ridgeline."

### Geometric Formations

- Later that night (0100-0300) saw distant lights grouping: "three or more lights group together, moved in a horizontal line, evenly spaced together or move towards another cluster to form a shape, I remember seeing diamonds or hexagons."

### Physical Object Sighting

- **Day 4**: At ~400 meters, saw "an object that was slightly darker than the sky at dusk and lighter than the color of the terrain." A faint red light was visible on the object. Object was static, hovering between two mountain ridges, "using the terrain to stay hidden." No sounds, no emissions, no movements despite heavy winds at that altitude.

### Later Sighting (Months Later)

- Yellow light at eye level, ~2,000 feet above ground, below ridgeline. Stationary initially, then moved north to south across ~16-mile distance over ~10 minutes.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- "Hatching" behavior with 5+ repetitions observed by naked eye and NVGs
- Nine-light static horizontal line formation
- Diamond and hexagon geometric light clusters (prolonged observation — witness "got bored looking at them")
- Chemtrail making a U-turn toward ground
- Physical object with red light, terrain-hiding behavior, no movement in heavy winds
