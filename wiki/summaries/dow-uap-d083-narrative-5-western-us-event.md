## Key Points

First-hand free-form narrative from Witness 5, the most geographically detailed of the witness accounts. Includes specific locations, coordinates [REDACTED], and hand-drawn sketches. Witness 5 worked with a partner (SA [REDACTED]) throughout.

### Night 1 (October 2023)

- Entered area via unpaved road near a wildlife national park area. Observed trailer, Jeep Wrangler, and old red Volkswagen van near entrance.
- **1858 hours**: Large orange light spotted ahead and slightly to the right. "Appeared to be placed on something, although nothing could be identified to be holding it up." Hovering with "zero resistance or movement," or suspended just over a distinctive peak in ridgeline. Lasted less than/about a minute.
- Coordinates noted: [REDACTED]
- **1918 hours**: Large red orb at further distance, facing north/northwest. Two red smaller orbs released simultaneously: one horizontally at steady speed, another upward with slight angle. Larger orb remained stationary during release. All lights gone in ~10 seconds. White flash of light in sky to the west accompanied.
- **1937 hours**: Large orb seen again to the left. Lasted ~10 seconds. Similar white flash, southwest.
- Set up camera ~3 miles from vehicle. Intermittent radio; other teams could not hear their responses.
- Heard other teams leave ~2330; began hike back.

### Night 2 (October 2023)

- Reached vehicle ~0130. Navigated out of wildlife area, reached gate ~0415.
- **~0430 hours**: Driving near a lake headed toward a base. Observed two lights ~2-3 feet above ground: one white and square, second round and red (diagonally above the white). Lights stayed over pavement on far side. Initially thought a bicycle.
- As they approached, lights drifted into sand off the road. When ~100 feet away, lights appeared to face them directly. As another vehicle approached from opposite direction, light pair "hovered, with zero resistance, or change in height, over the bushes" at ~15-20 mph. Lights stopped when they stopped their vehicle.
- Through NVGs saw the light pair "for a split second" before lights turned off. Then "a very thin line remain, maybe 4 ft wide, horizontal to the earth." Moved at same speed, 15-20 mph; rose in height while remaining a flat line.

### Translucent Object Encounter

- At ~0530, while searching the sighting location with NVGs and thermal binoculars, SA [REDACTED] reported seeing something described as a "cobweb."
- Witness 5 observed identical cobweb-like material floating nearby: one flowing down toward earth in a natural manner, the second moving *up* but "not with a natural flow."
- Through this, witness saw "a shape, not perfectly outlined, but faint lights in a perfect formation that moved together." Object appeared to be "moving up and slightly side to side" — giving "perception of trying to evade being noticed, or blend into the stars."
- After looking at a bright reference light and readjusting eyes, the exact same formation of faint lights appeared again but "at the opposite angle and at the same height and location."

### Ground Traces

- After sunrise, returned to the 0430 sighting location to search for anomalies.
- Found "odd scrapes in the ground around a certain plant." Appeared plant was susceptible to wind, but "it was not windy the night before." Dirt movement "looked very sharp and very fresh."
- "The sharp cut in the dirt was symmetrical and round, but did not always make a full circle." Same plant types at other times did not have similar markings regardless of size.
- Noted that tire tracks from another team's truck led back to their sighting location.
