## Key Points

This document is the **U.S. Army transmission cover memorandum** for the CIA Scientific Advisory Panel on Unidentified Flying Objects report, better known as the [[concepts/robertson-panel]] report. Based on the filename and known historical context:

- The Robertson Panel convened in January 1953 under the CIA's Office of Scientific Intelligence
- The panel, chaired by Dr. [[entities/h-p-robertson]], included physicists [[entities/luis-alvarez]] and [[entities/lloyd-berkner]], among others
- The panel concluded that UFO reports posed no direct threat to national security but recommended a debunking program to reduce public interest
- This Army transmission memo represents the **distribution channel** through which the Army received the CIA's scientific assessment

The document connects the Army's 1949 internal evaluation (see [[summaries/dow-uap-d084-usarmy-flying-saucer-study-1949]]) to the CIA's formal scientific panel, showing continuity in U.S. military interest in the flying disc phenomenon across agencies.
