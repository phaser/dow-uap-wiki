## Key Points

- Mission tasked under **Operation INHERENT RESOLVE**, AFCENT MAJCOM, USCENTCOM COCOM; Mission Type: XCAS (Cross-Coordination Close Air Support); Tasking Order "AB".
- Asset (callsign and tail redacted) launched at 060246Z MAY22, on station 0958Z–2036Z, landed 0004Z, last engine shutdown 0014Z (~21h 28m total mission).
- Operations center: 609 CAOC; reporting unit redacted; POC SSgt and QC SrA from 432 AEW (a unit historically associated with MQ-9 Reaper RPA at Creech AFB).
- Primary sensor FMV/SI; FMV exploited by DGS-1 (Distributed Common Ground System site 1, Langley AFB).
- Target development conducted on redacted Operation; tasked start point grid 38SMC54xx96xx (Iraq/Levant region).
- Weather: dust hindered most FMV collection of the ground.
- **5x UAPs observed in FOV from 1514Z to 1934Z**. The 1514Z UAP had the visual reconnaissance signature of a **possible [redacted] missile** transiting the field of view. The other 4 UAPs were assessed as "closer to the profile of possible birds."

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **6 May 2022, ~1514Z–1934Z, Iraq/Levant area, MGRS 38SMC53xx96xx** — RPA aircrew (likely 432 AEW MQ-9) observed five UAPs cross the FMV field of view over a four-and-a-half-hour window during target-development orbit. The 1514Z object visually resembled a missile in flight; the four follow-on objects fit a possible bird profile. Method: FMV.
