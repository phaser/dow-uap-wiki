## Key Points

- Mission flown by **196 ATKS** (196th Attack Squadron, California ANG, 163 AW, MQ-9 Reaper community at March ARB).
- Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**, ACC MAJCOM, USCENTCOM COCOM; Mission Type RECONNAISSANCE; Tasking Order "AP".
- Takeoff 0542Z (late, due to comm issues) from **OKAS** (Ali Al Salem Air Base, Kuwait); on station 0551Z–0005Z (next day); landed 0036Z; total mission 18h 54m.
- Targeting pod: **AN/DAS-4 (MTS-B)**; additional avionics: **AirHandler v2 (AHv2)** SIGINT system.
- Supported Marine Aviation Group ("MAG") under redacted "OP PHANTOM FLEX" (per page 1 narrative) ISR tasking near MGRS 38SMC36xx20xx (Iraq).
- FMV exploited by DGS-1.
- Weather: not a factor.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **20 May 2022, 2043Z, Iraq, MGRS 38SMC79xx70xx** — at 2043Z initial contact, the aircrew observed **1 UAP** flying north to north-east. The MQ-9 followed the object as long as possible; the DGS-1 screener could not get a positive ID. Friendly aircraft was at 18,000 ft at MGRS 38SMC85xx77xx at time of contact. UAP altitude/velocity unknown. MDS Type / Tail Number: UNK.
