## Key Points

- Mission flown by **89 ATKS** (89th Attack Squadron, 432 AEW — MQ-9 Reaper at Ellsworth AFB).
- Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**, AFCENT, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **ARMED RECCE**; Tasking Order "DI"; tasked by **TF CHOSIN** via 7-line.
- Take-off 1822Z 30JUL22 from **OJMS (Muwaffaq Salti AB, Jordan)**; on station 2028Z; off station 1409Z; landed 1519Z 31JUL22 at OJMS; total mission **20h 57m** (20.9 mission hours, 17.2 IMINT hours, 18.7 SIGINT hours).
- Operating area: **Eastern Syria** — Day Az Zar Parking Airfield region (37SFV grid), with extensive scans of MGRS cells in 37SFU2/3/4 (eastern Syria desert).
- ISR product: numerous ADM (adult male) follows, technical-vehicle observations, scans of "Compound 1" and "Compound 2," 2x TELARs with 3 rockets loaded (inactive) at Day Az Zar parking airfield, suspected shooting range with ~30 ADMs and rifle-bearing fighters at MGRS 37SFU2xx7xx.
