## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, AFRC, 20 FW — MQ-9 Reaper community).
- Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**, ACC MAJCOM, USCENTCOM COCOM; Mission Type **TARGET DEV / ISR**; Tasking Order "ID".
- Primary sensor FMV; sensors available "AH/BLASPHEMY" — AirHandler and BLASPHEMY (a SIGINT collection suite).
- Take-off 1206Z from **OKAS** (Ali Al Salem AB, Kuwait); on station 1220Z–0655Z next day; landed 0723Z; total mission 19h 17m.
- ISR conducted in support of redacted Task Force ("TF [redacted]") on Operation [redacted] near Baghdad, Iraq, MGRS 38SMB42xx83xx (Baghdad area). Activities: scans for HVI/POI, POL, ADMs in suits, weapons, convoys/sentry, personnel.
- FMV exploited by DGS-AR (DGS at Beale AFB).
- Intel gap filled? **No.** Weather: not a factor.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **1 December 2022, 1620Z, vicinity Baghdad, Iraq, MGRS 38SMB42xx83xx** — at FL180 (18,000 ft), the MQ-9 (callsign and tail redacted) observed **1x possible UAP/UAV flying west to east** in the vicinity of the friendly aircraft's position. The aircrew did **not follow the UAP** and continued the tasked mission; no further events observed. UAP signatures: none detected; UAP RF: none recorded; UAP effects on persons: none reported.
