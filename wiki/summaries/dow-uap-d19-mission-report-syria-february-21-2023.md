## Key Points

- Mission flown by **389 EFS / 332 AEW** — 389th Expeditionary Fighter Squadron, F-15E Strike Eagle 2-ship from **Muwaffaq Salti Air Base, Jordan (OJMS)**, ISO Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**.
- Mission Type: **DCA** (Defensive Counter-Air); JTAC callsign **KINGPIN**; Tasking Order "LG"; killbox ESSA (Syria).
- Aircraft tail numbers **169, 188**; loadout: 2x AIM-120D, 2x AIM-9X, M61A1 with 510 rounds, RR-180 chaff, MJU-51/53 flares; **Sniper-SE targeting pod**, AN/APG-82 radar (Suite 9.1), ALR-56C RWR, ALE-45 CMD.
- Takeoff 2320Z 20FEB23; on-station 0003Z 21FEB23; off-station 0340Z; landed 0425Z; total 4h 55m. Multiple air refuels (MOM track 37K lbs, GANDER track 33.5K + 11K lbs).
- **EMI event 0021Z–0024Z**: received MFT (multi-function targeting?) **radar jamming** vicinity Shaddadi, Syria, at FL270. JSIR Report ID 340377. Affected system: APG-82 (8.8–9.9 GHz). Working theory recorded in the document: **"area of effect Turkish X-band jammer on or across the SYR/TUR border into Turkey."**
- Two distinct UAP/AIRSIGHT events described (see below).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **AIRSIGHT #1 — 21 Feb 2023, 0025Z, vicinity Shaddadi, Syria, FL240** — F-15E flight (callsign redacted) was cruising in CAP at FL270 when crew **observed 3x possible UAP** at FL240 (~24,000 ft). **No radar returns received from UAP. No health effects experienced by aircrew. 2 white objects, IR significant.** (Document text says "3x POSS UAP" but only 2 are described as IR-significant.) Weather Sensitive Video (WSV) was produced. NFTR (no further to report). Weather: cloudy.
- **AIRSIGHT #2 — 21 Feb 2023, 0135Z, vicinity Shaddadi, Syria, FL210** — same flight observed **1x possible balloon** at ~21,000 ft. WSV produced. NFTR. Weather: cloudy. Aircraft type field recorded as **BALLOON**.
