## Key Points

- Mission flown by **77 EFS / 378 AEW** — 77th Expeditionary Fighter Squadron, F-16CM 2-ship out of **Prince Sultan AB (OEPS)**, Saudi Arabia.
- Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**, AFCENT, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **DCA** (Defensive Counter-Air); Tasking Order "MT"; killbox **ESSA** (Syria); JTAC callsign **KINGPIN**.
- Take-off 1901Z 31MAR23 from OEPS; transited via I2 (Iraq) airspace; entered Syrian (SY) airspace 2058Z; off-station 0000Z; landed 0148Z 1APR23.
- Loadout (per F-16): 2x AIM-120, 2x AIM-9X, 60 RR-180 chaff, 45 MJU-53/-66 flares, 2 ALE-50 towed decoys, M61A1 with 510 rds, **Sniper LDP**, HTS-P R7.85 (HARM Targeting System), Link 16, ALR-56M RWR, ALQ-184 ECM, ALQ-213 CMD, AN/APG-?? radar (software 8070 / MMC 7.3).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **31 March 2023, 2302:05Z, vicinity Syria, FL265, NE of "RLZ"** — F-16CM 2-ship at FL265, 293 KIAS heading, position MGRS 37SGA1xx. Crew observed **possible 10–20 UAPs** (record explicitly: "Number of UAP Sighted: POSS 10x–20x"; UAP altitude estimated **FL600+** — i.e., 60,000+ ft). Description: "OBSERVED SEVERAL BRIGHT OBJECTS MANUEVERING QUICKLY WEST TO EAST NE OF RLZ. FLT WOULD OBTAIN POSS UAP ON TARGETING POD (TV MODE) FOR APPROX 20S BEFORE OBJECT WOULD BECOME DIM AND DISAPPEAR FROM TARGETING POD. NONE OF THE OBJECTS WERE SEEN IN ANY FORMATIONS AS PREVIOUSLY SEEN IN PRIOR SORTIES." **Analyst comment: "FLT compared targeting pod between poss UAP and star. Results were different."** UAP signatures: none. UAP effects on persons: none.
