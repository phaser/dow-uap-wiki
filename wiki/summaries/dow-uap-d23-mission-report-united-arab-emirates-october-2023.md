## Key Points

- Mission flown by **50 ATKS** (50th Attack Squadron, 432 AEW — MQ-9 Reaper).
- Operation **OP SPARTAN SHIELD**, ACC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **ISR**; Tasking Order "UR".
- Take-off 0015Z 24OCT23 from **OMAM (Al Dhafra AB, UAE)**; on-station SRO track **IRISH SICKLE** at 0155Z; conducted IMINT 0155Z–1837Z; landed OMAM 2058Z; total 20h 43m.
- Targeting pod: AN/DAS-1; additional avionics: **AH/GMESH** (AirHandler + GMESH SIGINT). FMV exploited by DGS-2.
- "Professional guard call" received at 0145Z (likely civil-aviation guard frequency communication).
- Two UAP events observed during ISR orbit, both in northern Iraq grid (39RXK / 39RXL grid zone — vicinity Erbil / Kurdistan / Irbil region).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **UAP 1 — 24 Oct 2023, 0241Z, MGRS 39RXK6xx (northern Iraq region)** — friendly aircraft FL243, 162 KTAS, heading 280°. UAP observed for ~? duration, kinetic velocity estimate **320 mph**. UAP Physical State: Solid. UAP Signatures: **THERMAL SHOWED COLD** (i.e., the UAP was cooler than its surroundings on IR — opposite of "white-hot" reports). Maneuvering observations: NONE. Observer assessment: Benign. UAP Under Intelligent Control: NO. Effects on persons/equipment: none.
- **UAP 2 — 24 Oct 2023, 0322Z, MGRS 39RXL6xx (northern Iraq region)** — friendly aircraft same FL243, 162 KTAS, heading 280°. **Kinetic velocity: 440 mph.** UAP Physical State: Solid; UAP Signatures: UNK. Same boilerplate fields otherwise. Two distinct kinetic-velocity readings (320 → 440 mph) within 41 minutes suggest two different objects or one accelerating object.

## Open Questions
