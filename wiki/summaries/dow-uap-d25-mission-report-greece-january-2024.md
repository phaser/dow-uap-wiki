## Key Points

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **25 January 2024, 0509Z–0511Z, en-route to ISR area** — friendly aircraft (MQ-9) at FL250, heading 162°, 176 kts; position MGRS 35SQT67xx (Mediterranean / Cyprus area). Crew observed **1x UAP while in transit**. Initial observation: UAP flying at **~434 knots** (kinetic velocity estimate). **UAP description: "ROUND DIAMOND SHAPE WITH STRAIGHT, NON MANUEVERABLE 'TAIL' / non-manuevering probe at the bottom."** UAP appeared **only on the SWIR (short-wave infrared) camera**, not visible on other sensors. Event lasted **approx 2 minutes**. UAP Anomalous Characteristics: **"MAINTAINED STEADY FLIGHT PATH, INCREASED AND DECREASED ALTITUDE PROFILE, DID NOT CHANGE TRAJECTORY."** UAP Event Serial: **250509ZJAN2024-CENTCOM 001**. Observer: SrA (E-4); name redacted. UAP Last seen MGRS 35SPT63xx.
