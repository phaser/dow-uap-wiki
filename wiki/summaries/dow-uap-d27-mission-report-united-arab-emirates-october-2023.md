## Key Points

- Mission flown by **3 SOS** (3rd Special Operations Squadron, 27 SOW — MQ-9 Reaper / AFSOC).
- Operation **ENDURING SENTINEL**, AFSOC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **ISR**; Tasking Order "DK".
- Take-off 2100Z 6JUN24 from **OMAM (Al Dhafra AB, UAE)** via SLR (split-launch-recovery); 7-lined at 1822Z 6JUN24 but did **not arrive on station due to weather** — RTB.
- Two NIB (Non-Interference Basis) tasks performed: 2256Z and 0444Z, scanning for **dhows** (small Arabian Sea craft) at MGRS 40RFM63xx with EEIs around dhow flag status, equipment, weapons, cargo. JTAC: **ITC**.
- Targeting pod AN/DAS-1; additional avionics: AH/GMESH/SF; Link 16.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **7 June 2024, 0457Z, during RTB, vicinity MGRS 40RFM60xx (Arabian Sea / Strait of Hormuz approach)** — friendly aircraft (MQ-9) at **23,999 ft MSL**, 163 kts, transiting. Crew observed **1x UAP**. Description (verbatim): **"GLOWING HOT SPHERICAL UNIDENTIFIED OBJECT WITH A VERTICAL UNWAVERING CYLINDRICAL POLE/BAR ATTACHED ON THE BOTTOM OF THE OBJECT, POSS REFLECTION FROM THE OBJECT IN THE WATER, MOVING AT 140 KNOTS."** Kinetic velocity: 140 knots. First-accuracy: Precise. Observer reaction: NO engagement. UAP did not respond to observer.

## Open Questions

- Filename says October 2023; document is June 2024. Either the dossier filename was mislabeled at release, or the file substituted post-release.
- The "sphere + cylindrical pole/bar" matches the [[summaries/dow-uap-d25-mission-report-greece-january-2024]] "diamond shape with non-maneuverable tail/probe" — strongly suggests this is a recurring UAP form-factor class in the CENTCOM AOR. Synthesis page recommended.
- Reflection in water: implies the object was over the sea at low altitude; the 140-kt speed is consistent with a slow drone or a small aircraft, but the morphology is anomalous.
