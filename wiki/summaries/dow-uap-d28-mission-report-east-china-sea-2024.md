## Key Points

- Mission flown by **SOTU 016** (Special Operations Task Unit 016) under **16 SOS / 27 SOW** (16th Special Operations Squadron — AC-130J Ghostrider community at Cannon AFB).
- Operation **INHERENT RESOLVE**, AFSOC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **ARMED OVERWATCH**; Tasking Order "HM".
- Take-off 1740Z 20SEP24 from **OKAS (Ali Al Salem AB, Kuwait)**; arrived on-station 1930Z; off-station 2323Z; landed OKAS 0046Z. Total mission 7h 6m, on-station 3h 53m.
- Aircraft loadout: 105mm gun, 30mm gun (M61A1), AGM-176 Griffin missile, ALE-47 CMD with **716 RR-180 chaff and 240 MJU-71/-66/M206 flares**. Sensors: **MX-25** primary targeting; AAR-47 MWS, AN/AAQ-24 IRCM. **Aircraft is an AC-130J Ghostrider gunship.**
- During mission entered AAAB **ROZ RAINDROP** at 1930Z; conducted **weapons calibration** — released **20× 105mm, 101× 30mm, 1× AGM-176**.
- Received "call for fire" at 2025Z; employed AGM-176.
- Total munitions burn: 1020× 30mm and 80× 105mm (per Gentext on AC equipment line).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **20 September 2024, 2027:59Z, Ayn al Asad Airbase ROZ RAINDROP, MGRS 38SKC55xx 5xxx** — friendly aircraft (AC-130J) at **FL130** (13,000 ft), heading 096°, straight and level. **UAP flew through the aircraft sensors** (MX-20 & MX-25) at high rate of speed **between AGM-176 munition release and munition impact**. Observed by the **Weapons Systems Officer (WSO) and Combat Systems Officer (CSO)**. UAP description: **"UAP CREATED IR LENS FLARE ON MX-20 & MX-25 SENSORS, INDICATING A SIGNIFICANT HEAT SOURCE. THE UAP MOVED AT A HIGH RATE OF SPEED THROUGH THE SENSOR FIELD OF VIEW. THE CREW MAINTAINED LASER ENERGY UNTIL THE MUNITION IMPACTED ITS DESIRED TARGET. [REDACTED] DID NOT REOBSERVE THE UAP."** UAP Anomalous Characteristics: **"It is unknown at this time whether an object detached itself from the primary UAP immediately before leaving the sensor field of view."** UAP did not appear to respond to detection.
- This is one of the most operationally relevant UAP cases in the batch: a **UAP intersected the laser/sensor line of an AC-130 in the middle of an active weapons engagement**. The munition still hit its target, but the event constitutes a literal range-fouler under combat conditions.
