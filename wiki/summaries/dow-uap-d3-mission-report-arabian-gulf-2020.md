## Key Points

- ISR mission flown under AFCENT tasking; aircraft type, callsign, and basing fully redacted.
- 4 UAPs observed within the aircraft sensor field-of-view (FOV) over a roughly 27-second window starting 1736:22Z.
- Cloud coverage prevented the friendly aircraft from getting clear visual track of the objects after initial sighting.
- The Misrep template explicitly logs UAPs: "UAP Advanced Capabilities And/Or Materials" answered NO; most other UAP fields (propulsion, signatures, intelligent control, RF) left blank.
- POC, QC, and Approver were all U.S. Air Force; emails on the SIPR domain (`@mail.smil.mil`).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **2020 — Arabian Gulf, ~1736Z** — 4 UAPs observed by an AFCENT ISR asset. Sequence: 1 UAP at 17:36:22Z, 2 UAPs side-by-side at 17:36:30Z, 1 UAP at 17:36:49Z. The objects were observed beneath an obscuring cloud layer; the friendly platform could not maintain tracking. No physical-state, propulsion, or signature data captured.
