## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **March 2023 (specific date redacted), Pacific Time Zone, near a national security facility, night** — civilian witness reported (paraphrased from IIR summary):
  - Object: **large blue, featureless, triangular** with a **solid, unwavering silhouette**.
  - Behavior: emitting **powerful "whitish blue" light from multiple points along its perimeter**. **Hovered stationary** above or near the facility for **~3 minutes**, then moved to a higher position in field of view.
  - Anomalous motion: characterized as **"backing up" in a "jerking" or "jumping" manner**, **inconsistent with smooth jet propulsion**.
  - Total observation: ~8 minutes.
  - Witness could not determine: control mechanism, propulsion, "front or rear," flight path, recording capability, vapor trail, or cloaking — and "didn't think" it was a drone.
