## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **4 September 2020, 21:09Z–21:17Z, Gulf of Aden, MGRS 38PL7?xx** — narrative: *"Contact at 21:09z to 21:17z 04SEP2020. While at 23,819 HAT over the Gulf of Aden we tracked a **round, cold object in IR** traveling **168 degrees at 277 mph**. It made a few **abrupt directional changes during the 8 minute contact**. Our sensor was aimed –39° below our altitude with a slant range of 6.17 NM and ground range of 8.81 km. The IR sensor was set to **black hot** and the object in question was a **bright white**."*
- Round, IR-cold (bright white on black-hot mode = colder than background), traveling 168° at 277 mph at low-mid altitude over the Gulf of Aden. Abrupt directional changes during 8 minutes.
