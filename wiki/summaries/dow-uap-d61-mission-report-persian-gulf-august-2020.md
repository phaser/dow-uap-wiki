## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, MQ-9 Reaper, AFRC, 432 AEW).
- ACC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **AREC**; supported **NAVCENT** on redacted operation.
- Take-off 2307Z 26AUG20 from **OKAS**; on station 2320Z; off station 1944Z 27AUG20; landed 2012Z; total mission 21h 5m. 17.9 IMINT hours, 19.2 SIGINT hours.
- Targeting pod ANDAS-4; additional avionics AH/GMESH. FMV exploited by DGS-1.
- Tasked Start Point MGRS 39RUN46xx — Strait of Hormuz / Arabian Gulf.
- ISR objectives: characterize IRIN/IRGCN vessels, UAS activity, activity outside of ports, establish pattern of life.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **GUARDCALL 1 — 27 Aug 2020, 0532Z, MGRS 40RCP7xx, FL150, heading 018° M** — MQ-9 hailed on guard frequency by **Iranian Air Defense**. Standard call; standard response; Professional tone; no mission impact. (Mirrors D60 GUARDCALL.)
- **Maritime observation, 0611Z** — observed 1 small vessel docked at **Greater Tunb naval port** (UAE-claimed island occupied by Iran), MGRS 40RCQ3xx.
- **OBSERVATION / UAP FORMATION 1 — 27 Aug 2020, 1527Z–1529Z, MGRS 39RVM8xx (Arabian Gulf coast)** — **GENTEXT/OBSERVATION (S//REL)**: *"From 1527Z to 1529Z [callsign] **observed a formation of flying objects traveling NE-NW along the coast** IVO 39RVM8xx. [Callsign] was tracking this formation for approximately 2 minutes before PID was lost in cloud cover. Aircrew was unable to gain PID again on this formation."* Method of Observation: **MX-?? sensor / FMV**. Observed Activity Description: **FORMATION OF UNK FLYING OBJECTS.** Weather: light cloud coverage prevented continuous tracking of UAP event.
- **Maritime observation, 1742Z** — 1x probable Naser WAP (Iranian Naser-class missile boat) alongside an M/T (motor tanker), both DIW (dead in water), with 2x ADMs on the rear deck of the Naser.
- **Maritime observation, 1700Z** — 1x possible **Il-76 Candid** transport aircraft at **Abu Musa Island airfield**, MGRS 40RCP0xx.
