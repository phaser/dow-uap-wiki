## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, MQ-9 Reaper).
- ACC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **AREC**; supported **NAVCENT** on redacted operation.
- Take-off 2302Z 15SEP20 from **OKAS**; on station 2313Z; supported NAVCENT 0318Z–1829Z; landed 1958Z; total 20.9 mission hours, 15.2 IMINT, 19.3 SIGINT.
- Three Iranian Air Defense **guard calls** (0408Z, 0421Z — both DIRECTIVE tone; 1141Z — PROFESSIONAL).
- **Two lost-link / EMI events**:
  - **1248Z–1259Z** (11 min): Aircraft at MGRS 39RXK0xx5xx, FL180, 89 KIAS. Lost link to a **possible [redacted] (1.4g)**. Complete impact; flight path deviation; mission impact MEDIUM. JSIR ID 330412.
  - **1414Z–1441Z** (27 min): Aircraft at MGRS 39RVM9xx, FL180, 120 KIAS, heading 264°. Same possible source. JSIR ID 330414.
- Both EMI/lost-link events occurred several hours **before** the UAP observation at 1732Z, but in the same mission and same general area (Strait of Hormuz region).

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **GUARDCALLs 1, 2, 3 — 16 Sep 2020, 0408Z / 0421Z / 1141Z** — three Iranian Air Defense hails on guard frequency (243.000 MHz at 1141Z); two with DIRECTIVE tone (early morning), one PROFESSIONAL (late morning). All received standard responses; no mission impact.
- **EMI / Lost-Link 1 — 1248Z, MGRS 39RXK0xx, FL180** — 11-minute total link loss during NAVCENT-supporting orbit. Source: possible [redacted].
- **EMI / Lost-Link 2 — 1414Z, MGRS 39RVM9xx, FL180** — 27-minute total link loss; same possible source.
- **OBSERVATION / UAP — 16 Sep 2020, 1732Z, MGRS 39RVM5xx, FL180, 90 KIAS** — *"AT 1732Z [callsign redacted] OBSERVED A UAP IVO 39RVM5xx [further details under 1.4a redaction]."* Method of observation: FMV. Observed Activity Description: UAP.
