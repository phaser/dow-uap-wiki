## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, MQ-9).
- AFCENT, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **AREC** (likely); supported **NAVCENT** on redacted operation IVO **Arabian Gulf, Strait of Hormuz, and Gulf of Oman**. Total mission ~17.3 IMINT hours, ~15.3 SIGINT hours, 4 total taskings prosecuted.
- Take-off 2249Z 1OCT20 from **OKAS**; on station 2300Z; landed OKAS 1835Z 2OCT20.
- **Five Iranian Air Defense guard calls** (08542Z, 1122Z, 1236Z, 1315Z, 1825Z) — even more guard-call dense than D62.
- 1922Z UAP observation explicitly recorded.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- Multiple GUARDCALLs by Iranian Air Defense: 0854Z, 1122Z (DIRECTIVE), 1236Z (PROFESSIONAL), 1315Z, and 1825Z. Aircraft positions across MGRS 40RCP (Strait of Hormuz approaches) at FL120–FL160. All standard responses given; no mission impact.
- **OBSERVATION / UAP — 2 Oct 2020, 1922Z** — narrative records: *"AT 1922Z [callsign] OBSERVED 1X UAP."* Specific MGRS, altitude, and kinematic fields redacted under 1.4a.
