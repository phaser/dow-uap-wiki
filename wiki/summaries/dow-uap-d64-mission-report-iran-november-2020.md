## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, MQ-9 Reaper).
- ACC, USCENTCOM; Mission Type **AREC**; supported **NAVCENT** on redacted operation IVO Arabian Gulf, Strait of Hormuz, Gulf of Oman.
- Take-off 0608Z 2NOV20 from **OKAS**; landed OKAS 0250Z 3NOV20; total mission **20.42 hours**; 18.55 IMINT hours.
- ISR objectives: scans IAW target deck in Arabian Gulf; **open-water scans ISO UUV** (uncrewed underwater vehicle search) 1910Z–2210Z, no UUV identified.
- **One Iranian Air Defense GUARDCALL** at 1012Z, MGRS 39RUN[redacted], FL210, heading 068°M; PROFESSIONAL tone; standard response; no mission impact.
- **Two UAP observations** within 5 minutes of each other.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **OBSERVATION 1 / UAP — 2 Nov 2020, 2143Z, MGRS 39RWK1xx, FL220, 105 KIAS, heading 110°T** — *"AT 2143Z [callsign] [1.4a] OBSERVED 1X UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON IVO 39RWK[redacted] ALTITUDE UNKNOWN WITH A BEARING OF 080T."*
- **OBSERVATION 2 / UAP — 2 Nov 2020, 2148Z, MGRS 39RWK[redacted], FL220, 107 KIAS, heading 110°T** — *"AT 2148Z [callsign] OBSERVED AN ADDITIONAL UAP TRAVELING NW IVO 39RWK[redacted]."*
- Two UAPs, same area (39RWK grid — Persian Gulf, vicinity Iranian coast), 5 minutes apart, both observed via FMV. Suggests either two passes on the same object, two members of the same swarm, or two distinct objects in a small area.
