## Key Points

- Mission flown by **482 ATKS** (482nd Attack Squadron, MQ-9 Reaper). The 482 ATKS pattern: D60 (Aug 7), D61 (Aug 26), D62 (Sep 15), D63 (Oct 1), D64 (Nov 2), D65 (Jul 16) — all NAVCENT-supporting, all with UAP encounters.
- AFCENT-style; ACC; USCENTCOM; Mission Type **AREC**; supported **NAVCENT** on redacted operation IVO Arabian Gulf, Strait of Hormuz, Gulf of Oman.
- Take-off 0443Z 16JUL20 from **OKAS**; on station 0504Z; landed 0210Z 17JUL20; total mission **21h 17m**; 18.3 IMINT hours, 1 IMINT tasking, 18.3 [SIGINT?] hours, 1 [SIGINT?] tasking, 2 total.
- One Iranian Air Defense guard call recorded (0552Z).
- **Three UAP observations** noted in the narrative.

## Notable Incidents / Cases

- **OBSERVATION 1 / UAP — 16 Jul 2020, 1830Z** — *"OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON. SEE OBS LINE 1."*
- **OBSERVATION 2 / UAP — 16 Jul 2020, 1909Z** — *"OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON. SEE OBS LINE 2."*
- **OBSERVATION 3 / UAP — 16 Jul 2020, 2345Z, MGRS 39RUN6234236874 (Persian Gulf, off Iranian coast), FL191, 115 KIAS, heading 331°M** — *"AT 2345Z [callsign] OBSERVED AN UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL PHENOMENON IVO 39RUN6234236874."* Method: FMV.
- This is the first known mission in the 482 ATKS NAVCENT series with **three** UAP events in a single sortie — earlier in the temporal pattern (mid-July) than the August/September/October/November cluster.
