## Open Questions

- The intermittent "two blobs" vs. "one elongated smear" appearance across the zoom segment is the strongest argument that the apparent pairing is real and not an optical fixed artifact: a fixed artifact would not change shape between frames. The change of appearance is consistent with a real pair whose relative aspect or separation varies in time.
