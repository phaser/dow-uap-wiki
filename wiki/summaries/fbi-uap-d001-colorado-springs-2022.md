## Key Points

- Witness [REDACTED] was interviewed via Microsoft Teams in March 2025 by FBI Special Agent and Supervisory Special Agent
- Witness was a U.S. Army soldier at Fort Carson
- While walking with **four other soldiers** toward a building on Fort Carson, all five men observed a UAP hovering just over Cheyenne Mountain
- Weather was pleasant with clear blue skies
- The object was **matte white/off-white, non-metallic, oval-shaped, horizontal**, with a **curved indentation on the bottom** — described as "bean-shaped"
- The object was completely **motionless** and **silent**
- Covered in **intersecting lines or ridges forming an abstract polygon pattern** across the entire object
- Size: about "a pool cue at arm's length" or roughly 10% of the mountain saddle width
- Observed for approximately **3–5 minutes**; did not change position relative to the mountain saddle (ruling out wind drift)
- All five witnesses **looked away momentarily** to decide who would retrieve a phone from the vehicle to record video; when they looked back, **the object had vanished**
- They searched the western skyline but could not reacquire the object
- Each man independently drew what they remembered; **all drawings were consistent** — horizontal, white, bean-shaped with abstract crossing lines
- The witness believed the incident was officially reported to the Army
- **AARO contacted the witness in early 2024** about the observation; the witness provided a response but was **never interviewed by AARO or anyone else** prior to this FBI interview
