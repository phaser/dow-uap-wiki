## Key Points

- Witness [REDACTED] was a **former U.S. Army intelligence officer** who, along with **four other members of his unit**, observed a UAP over the Cheyenne Mountains as they exited their office building
- Day described as "perfect 'blue bird' sky" — no clouds, little humidity, temperature ~50°F
- Object described as **"potato" shaped** with distinct edges, painted in a **creamy/whitish opalescent color**, **somewhat translucent with a slight shimmer**
- The object was made up of what can best be described as **articulating fish scales or panels** — non-symmetrical, non-overlapping, irregular-shaped panels
- The object itself was **perfectly still**, but **each panel on the object shifted in slow waves** starting at different points of origin but at the same time
- After approximately **two minutes**, the object vanished. The witness specifically stated the object **"cloaked"** — disappeared in the time it took to turn a head, and **there was no shadow**
- Forensic sketch artist conducted the interview to produce a sketch of the object; sketch drafts attached
- Artist was to relay additional drafts through the SA to the witness to complete the final sketch product
