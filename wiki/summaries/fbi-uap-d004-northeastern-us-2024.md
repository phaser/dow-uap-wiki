## Key Points

- Witness [REDACTED] provided **multiple video clips** showing objects appearing to be unknown UxS or UAP near their residence
- First observation: **early morning hours of November 2021**
- Objects described as **"plasma orbs"** by the FBI agent documenting the contact
- Objects observed as close as **200 yards** from the residence
- Behavior: hovering in static position, and moving; sometimes above the horizon, sometimes **close to the ground, within trees, and in or near water**
- Witness privately consulted with multiple people about observations
- Set up **trail cameras and other sensors** to collect data from property
- Witness reported **spikes in Gamma radiation that correlate with times of observed objects**
- Additional reports of **unusual effects on electronics and GPS data** from devices
- Witness was never interested in UAP topic prior to November 2021; described self as "a skeptic" before observations
- Has lived at residence since **2006** and had never seen anomalous lights or activity behind property prior to November 2021
- **Neighbor to the south** stated they had also seen strange lights in the tree line behind their house
- Witness rarely witnessed any human activity in the nature area near residence; no activity that would explain observations
- Witness wants to protect family's privacy; does not want information to become public
- Proposed in-person meeting likely did not occur due to travel plans
