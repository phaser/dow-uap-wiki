## Key Points

- FBI Special Agents conducted a **site survey** of the witness's property (same witness from [[summaries/fbi-uap-d004-northeastern-us-2024]] and [[summaries/fbi-uap-d005-northeastern-us-2024]])
- Witness provided a tour of property, giving perspective to previously provided recordings
- Agents were taken into a **wooded area approximately 200 yards** from the home where a **trail camera** is affixed — this camera has captured UAP activity facing west
- Within the wooded area: signs indicating area restricted from hunting/fishing; a **stone wall running south to north**
- Witness indicated a trail goes around but **does not run through the area where UAP activity has been observed**
- **Multiple neighboring homes** along a road to the east would have **line of sight** to the observation area
- Witness showed a video of walking in the vicinity where UAP activity was observed
- Witness gave agents a copy of [REDACTED] and stated they would be **open to equipment being set up** on property for recording or time-lapse video
- Witness willing to coordinate with agents for **evening visits** when UAP activity is often observed
- Enclosures include: 14 photos of tree lines west of home, 6 photos from trail camera on property, 4 additional photos, and a photo log
