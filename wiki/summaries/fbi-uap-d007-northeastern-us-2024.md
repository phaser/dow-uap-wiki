## Key Points

- **Two FBI Special Agents** visited the home of the witness (same witness from D004–D006) to confirm reports and video
- Weather: ~48°F, overcast, no precipitation; official sunset time [REDACTED]
- At approximately **4:45 PM**: Agents and witness went to the patio on the north side of the home. Witness pointed out distant permanent lights and claimed light sources ~8 miles away they couldn't identify

**First Agent Observation (~5:00 PM)**:

- SA [REDACTED] observed a **white pulsation of light** appear in the direction of [REDACTED], at the northern edge of [REDACTED]
- Light moved **horizontally with somewhat erratic movements**
- Light did **not reflect off the water** below — suggesting the source was over the wooded area north of the water
- Light did **not illuminate the area below** or the water surface
- Light appeared at or below the **tree line** altitude
- White light "danced around" for less than ten seconds, moving side to side, then disappeared

**Multiple Additional Observations**:

- Within the tree line to the west, Agents observed **white lights appearing at the top of the tree line** — these pulsed and flashed with a white/blue hue, then disappeared
- These lights were more stationary (less erratic movement than the first observation)
- SA also observed **plumes of broad light** appearing for several seconds before a flash of light (either red or white) moved erratically before disappearing
- Five photos taken but mostly blurry — tripod needed for clear images due to long exposure

**Second Agent Observation (~5:30 PM)**:

- Both SAs observed a UAP: **bright white light above a bright red light** at the far end of the pond
- Hovered at approximately **tree-top height**
- Moved quickly from **right to left, disappeared, then re-appeared simultaneously where it had first appeared**, moved quickly right to left and disappeared again
- Both Agents observed this activity
- Agents remained observing until ~6:10 PM; witness [REDACTED] and [REDACTED] arrived home at ~5:15 PM and joined observation
- Agents departed at ~6:15 PM

## Notable Incidents / Cases
