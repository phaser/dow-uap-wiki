## Key Points

- Couple [REDACTED] interviewed together via Microsoft Teams by FBI agents
- Witness [REDACTED] holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics & Finance; works as [REDACTED]; currently the [REDACTED]
- Arrived home ~9:15 PM EST; while pulling into the downhill driveway, noticed an **intense bright light hovering below the tree line** in the middle of their backyard
- Partner [REDACTED] was inside, checked security camera, saw witness standing next to vehicle looking into backyard, and came outside
- Object described as a **red sphere approximately one meter in diameter**
- Red color described as "**brilliant and beautiful**" — the witness "had never seen anything that color of red before"
- Inside the red sphere, at the center: a **"white plasma sun" about the size of a basketball**
- As they watched, the orb moved up and to their left; then they noticed a **second, identical orb** hovering above the first
- The two orbs moved together **toward the west above the tree line**
- From start of observation to when orbs traveled out of sight, they exhibited **both a change in altitude and a change in direction**
- Orbs were **silent** and moved in a **smooth motion**
- Moved **in tandem as if flying in formation or tethered together**
- As they moved out of sight: the witness saw the orbs **merge together into one orb**, or were so close together they appeared as one
- Video captured beginning at the point when the orbs were above the trees and moving away from the backyard; **video is attached** to the interview document
- **Follow-up sighting**: A few weeks later, witness saw **several white orbs travel over their house from west to east**, at **much higher altitude** than the prior red orbs — described as "the size of a dime held at arm's length"
