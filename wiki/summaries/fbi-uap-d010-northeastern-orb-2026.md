## Key Points

- Witness [REDACTED] interviewed along with [REDACTED]
- Arrived home in the evening around **9:15 PM**
- While in the driveway, noticed a **ball of light** through the windshield — **yellow, about the size of a basketball**
- Upon exiting the vehicle, saw a **second ball of light** — **larger, about the size of a beach ball**
- Objects were behind a row of trees (since removed) but in front of another section of trees between the witness's property and the neighbor's property
- Witness was able to look at the lights without strain or discomfort on the eyes
- No health issues or concerns since seeing the objects
- **Agent note**: Based on witness's position, objects were approximately **30 yards away and 20–30 feet above the ground**
- Partner [REDACTED] came outside; both observed the objects move **laterally to their left**, passing behind a tree ~36 yards away, at the same height, continuing behind a neighbor's house and beyond a tree ~75 yards away
- At this point, witness saw **one orb going into the other orb before disappearing abruptly**
- **Follow-up sighting**: A few weeks later, saw **several white orbs travel over the house from west to east**, at much higher altitude than the prior red orbs
- **Earlier sighting (1987)**: Witness saw something strange near power lines at [REDACTED]. A **red light circling around itself** at night, described "like the front end of Kit from the TV show Knight Rider." Object hovered then **landed near the power station**. Since then, has always been interested in the UAP topic
