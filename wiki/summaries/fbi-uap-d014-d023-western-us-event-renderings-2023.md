## Key Points

### Organizational Structure
- The renderings are organized into **two incidents** (Incident 1 and Incident 2), each with multiple scene renderings
- **Incident 1**: Three renderings (D014, D015, D021) — D021 is at higher resolution (4800×2700) vs. the others (1852×1042)
- **Incident 2**: Seven renderings (D016–D020, D022–D023) — all from perspective of "Witness_01," with SCENE1 (5 images), SCENE2 (1 image), and SCENE3 (1 image)

### Photoshop Layer Naming Analysis
- The layer name `Witness_01-UAPs-SCENE1` (used on D016–D020) indicates these five images show **Scene 1 from Witness 01's perspective**, depicting multiple UAPs simultaneously
- `Witness_01-UAPs-SCENE2` (D023) shows the same witness's perspective of a separate scene (Scene 2)
- `Witness_01-UAPs-SCENE3` (D022) shows Scene 3
- The Incident 1 renderings (D014, D015, D021) lack embedded Photoshop layer metadata — may have been flattened before export, or produced by a different artist/workflow
- D021 (4800×2700) is the highest-resolution image in the set — suggests it may be a master or composite rendering

### Context in the Western US Event
These renderings correspond to the 2025 Western US Event documented in:
- [[summaries/western-us-event-slides-2026-05-08]] — four distinct sighting types: "Orbs Launching Orbs," "Large Fiery Orb," "Dark Kite," and "Transparent Kite"
- [[entities/western-us-helicopter-orb-2025]] — helicopter crew encounter with orbs within 10 feet
- The DOW batch narratives (D077–D083) which provide detailed textual accounts of the same Western US Event incidents

Based on the rendering count (3 for Incident 1, 7 for Incident 2):
- **Incident 1** likely corresponds to the **"Orbs Launching Orbs" and/or "Fiery Orb"** events (orange/red orb phenomenology)
- **Incident 2** likely corresponds to the **"Dark Kite" and "Transparent Kite"** events (triangular/kite-shaped object phenomenology with NVG/bare-eye dual observation)

The Photoshop layer name "Witness_01-UAPs-SCENE1" confirms these were produced as **forensic reconstructions** based on witness testimony — consistent with FBI forensic artist methodology (cf. D002–D003 for the Colorado Springs sketch workflow).
