## Key Points

- **First-person USPER narrative** from a senior U.S. intelligence officer (USIC = U.S. Intelligence Community). This is a rare direct-account primary source from an active-duty intelligence professional.
- **Mission context**: Investigated loud "thuds" heard in mountains on a test range, coinciding with UAP orb sightings over several nights. Searched for debris or objects.
- **Helicopter mission**: Deployed from a Joint Operations Center (JOC) with a colleague and two pilots. Flew low-altitude route through mountain range; spotted debris on ground each time, all determined to be remnants from years of weapons testing.
- **Cave discovery**: Found a large cave entrance with no visible end; no safe landing spot; orbited several times for observation.
- **Extended close encounter**: After refueling and re-deployment, radar detected hits several miles up-range. Pursued object(s) for **over an hour**. Ground teams reported a UAP as "super-hot," low to the ground, moving east then south at high speed — **the object then split into two and changed direction**.
- **Close helicopter encounter**: Object rose from ground, approached within **ten feet of the helicopter**, dropped below, and sped away. Pilots observed it through NVGs; saw it **split into two as a smaller object emerged** before accelerating out of sight. Pursuit broken off — "unable to match its speed."
- **Fighter jet interaction**: Several fighter jets launched on a training mission. The same type of orbs appeared **directly above the fighters**, flaring up one at a time in horizontal formation, **matching the jets' speed and flight path**, then dimming sequentially and disappearing. Repeated several times. Narrator remarked it seemed the orbs were "chasing" the fighters.
- **Orb swarm**: At ~700 ft AGL, observed **countless orange orbs swarming in all directions** against mountain backdrop. Display lasted several minutes before fading.
- **T-formation**: Two large orbs flared side by side, stationary just above rotor disk to the right. **Oval-shaped, orange with white/yellow center, emitting light in all directions.** A third orb flared below, then a fourth, forming a **"T" formation** of 4-5 orbs. Dimmed in reverse order, remained stationary until vanishing. Event lasted 10-15 seconds.
- **Triangle formation**: Orange orbs flaring up and down around the helicopter, forming a **distinct triangle** before vanishing.
- **Narrator did not take photos** — focused on assessing threat. Pilots and narrator were "virtually speechless."
