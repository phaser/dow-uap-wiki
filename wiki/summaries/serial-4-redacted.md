## Key Points

- Companion FD-302 to [[summaries/serial-3-redacted]] — same UAP event, separate witness; same investigating agents.
- Witness: a [redacted] drone pilot, "ten to fifteen hours as a drone pilot." Was at [redacted] with **three other contractors and [redacted] for LiDAR tests** with [redacted]. Interviewed via Facetime video while in [redacted].
- **September [redacted] 2023**: After receiving a [redacted] briefing, the five-person team got into three vehicles. The female witness from `serial-3` drove the first vehicle; this witness was her front-seat passenger.
- ~7:30 a.m.: Three vehicles began driving south. Sun in the east; good visibility.
- Convoy reached a gate "that was giving [redacted] trouble" (consistent with the gate fob malfunction in `serial-3`).
- "At about three quarters of the windshield up," the witness saw a **linear object with a super-bright light on the east side of the object**.
  - Light "bright white and bright enough to see bands within the light."
  - Object: **metallic / gray in color**; no wings, no exhaust.
  - Size: smaller than a 737, "one to two Blackhawk helicopters in length"; definitely bigger than a drone.
  - Altitude: approximately **5,000 ft above ground level**.
  - Motion: moved **east to west parallel to the ground**; visible 5-10 seconds; "then the light went out and the object vanished."
  - Sky was clear; could not reacquire after disappearance.
- Object stayed "the same size" and same light intensity throughout observation.
- He did not see object before going through the gate; saw it during gate transit.
- No vehicle interference observed.
- Other contractors: the female lead witness was "annoyed" about restricted-airspace incursion; another [redacted] in the second vehicle "tried to get [someone] to see the object but was unsuccessful."
- **Aftermath**: That night, a storm came through and the TV went out in the witness's hotel room. He was "still freaked out" and went downstairs to "make sure all the TVs were out in the hotel and not just his." The witness "had weird dreams and had trouble sleeping for the first two nights after he saw the object." (The "TVs out" episode is reasonably attributable to the storm but is recorded in the FD-302.)
